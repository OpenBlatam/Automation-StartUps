## ğŸ¯ **BEST PRACTICES**

### **Metrics Selection**
1. **Focus on What Matters**: Measure only critical metrics
2. **Balance Leading & Lagging**: Track both
3. **Keep It Simple**: Avoid metric overload
4. **Regular Reviews**: Check metrics consistently
5. **Action-Oriented**: Metrics must drive action

### **Dashboard Design**
1. **Visual Hierarchy**: Most important metrics first
2. **Color Coding**: Green/Yellow/Red indicators
3. **Trend Indicators**: Show direction (up/down)
4. **Comparisons**: Target vs. Actual
5. **Context**: Show why metrics matter
