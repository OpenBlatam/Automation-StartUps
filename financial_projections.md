## Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Revenue Risks
- **Course Completion Rates**: Target 68% (realistic vs. industry 45%)
- **SaaS Churn**: Target 6.2% monthly (realistic vs. industry 7.5%)
- **Seasonality**: Diversified revenue streams reduce impact
- **AI Model Costs**: Negotiate volume discounts, optimize prompts

### Cost Risks
- **AI Infrastructure Costs**: Negotiate volume discounts
- **Talent Acquisition**: Competitive equity packages
- **Marketing Efficiency**: Focus on organic growth channels
