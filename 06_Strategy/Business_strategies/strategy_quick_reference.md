## ðŸŽ¯ **QUICK DECISION FRAMEWORKS**

### **Strategy Selection Matrix**
Rate each option (1-10) on:
- **Impact**: How much will this achieve our goals?
- **Feasibility**: How likely can we succeed?
- **Speed**: How fast can we execute?
- **Cost**: What's the resource requirement?

**Choose the option with highest total score.**

### **Go/No-Go Criteria**
**Go if:**
- Aligns with strategic objectives
- Has clear success metrics
- Required resources available
- Team is committed

**No-Go if:**
- Distracts from priorities
- Success metrics unclear
- Resources stretched thin
- Team resistance

## ðŸŽ¯ **COMMON STRATEGIC ISSUES & SOLUTIONS**

### **Issue: Too Many Priorities**
**Solution**: Focus on top 3. Say no to everything else.

### **Issue: Strategy Not Executed**
**Solution**: Assign owners. Set deadlines. Review weekly.

### **Issue: Team Not Aligned**
**Solution**: Communicate vision clearly. Link daily work to strategy.

### **Issue: Slow Results**
**Solution**: Focus on leading indicators. Act on early signals.

### **Issue: External Change**
**Solution**: Monitor market. Be ready to pivot.

## âš¡ **EMERGENCY STRATEGIC DECISION MAKER**

### **Decision Criteria (Rate 1-5)**
1. **Strategic Fit** (5 = perfect, 1 = off strategy)
2. **Financial Impact** (5 = high value, 1 = low value)
3. **Feasibility** (5 = very doable, 1 = not doable)

**Decision Threshold**: Score 12+ points = Go
**Score 8-11** = Discuss more
**Score <8** = Don't do it
