## üìä **HOW TO USE THIS TEMPLATE**

### **Step 1: Gather Information**
- Review internal data
- Survey stakeholders
- Analyze market trends
- Monitor competitors

### **Step 2: Brainstorm**
- List all strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats
- Be honest and thorough
- Don't filter yet

### **Step 3: Prioritize**
- Focus on top 3-5 in each category
- Validate with data
- Get stakeholder input

### **Step 4: Strategy Development**
- Create SO, ST, WO, WT strategies
- Prioritize strategies
- Create action plans

### **Step 5: Execute & Review**
- Execute action plans
- Review quarterly
- Update SWOT annually
