## ðŸš€ **CASE 1: NETFLIX - DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION**

### **Challenge**
DVD rental company facing digital disruption.

### **Strategy**
Transform from DVD rentals to streaming platform.

### **Implementation**
1. **2007**: Launched streaming service
2. **2011**: Split DVD and streaming (controversial but necessary)
3. **2013**: Started original content with "House of Cards"
4. **2016+**: Global expansion and massive original content investment

### **Key Success Factors**
- Bold vision: World's best streaming platform
- Technology investment: Platform and infrastructure
- Content strategy: Original, exclusive content
- Data-driven: Used viewing data to guide decisions

### **Results**
- 200M+ subscribers globally
- $280B+ market cap
- 95% market share in some regions
- Disrupted traditional TV industry

### **Key Learnings**
1. **Be willing to disrupt yourself**: Netflix killed their DVD business
2. **Invest in technology**: Platform was key
3. **Data drives decisions**: Viewing patterns guided content
4. **Go global early**: International expansion accelerated growth
