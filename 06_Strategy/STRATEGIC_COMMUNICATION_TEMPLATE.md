## ðŸŽ¯ **COMMUNICATION BEST PRACTICES**

### **Message Development**
1. **Start with audience**: What do they need to know?
2. **Use simple language**: Avoid jargon and complexity
3. **Tell a story**: Make it memorable and engaging
4. **Include action**: What should they do next?
5. **Repeat key messages**: Reinforce important points

### **Delivery**
1. **Choose right channel**: Match message to medium
2. **Time appropriately**: When will audience be receptive?
3. **Use visuals**: Support messages with graphics
4. **Encourage interaction**: Ask questions, seek feedback
5. **Follow up**: Reinforce and clarify as needed

### **Measurement**
1. **Track engagement**: Open rates, attendance, participation
2. **Measure understanding**: Surveys, quizzes, discussions
3. **Assess action**: Are people doing what's expected?
4. **Monitor sentiment**: How do people feel about strategy?
5. **Adjust approach**: Improve based on feedback
