## ðŸŒŸ **VISION STATEMENT**

### **What is a Vision Statement?**
A vision statement describes your desired future state - where you want to be in 5-10 years.

**Characteristics**:
- Inspiring and aspirational
- Long-term focused (5-10 years)
- Clear and memorable
- Motivational
- Forward-looking

### **Vision Statement Template**

**Option 1**: 
*"To become the [position] in [industry/niche] by [measurable outcome], empowering [target audience] to [transformation]."*

**Option 2**:
*"By [year], we will be recognized as [position] by [what success looks like], [positive impact on stakeholders]."*

**Option 3**:
*"Our vision is to [transform/revolutionize] [industry/market] by [key achievement], creating [value/impact] for [target audience]."*

### **Vision Builder**

#### **Step 1: Future State** (10 min)
Write about your ideal future:

**In 10 years, our company will:**
- [What we'll be known for]
- [What we'll have achieved]
- [Impact we'll have made]
- [Position we'll hold]

#### **Step 2: Impact** (10 min)
Describe the impact you want to make:

**We will transform:**
- [Industry/Market]: [How]
- [Customers]: [How]
- [Society]: [How]

#### **Step 3: Values** (10 min)
Identify core values reflected in vision:

- **Value 1**: [How it's reflected]
- **Value 2**: [How it's reflected]
- **Value 3**: [How it's reflected]

## ðŸŽ¯ **MISSION STATEMENT**

### **What is a Mission Statement?**
A mission statement describes your purpose - why you exist and what you do.

**Characteristics**:
- Clear and specific
- Focused on present
- Describes what you do
- Defines who you serve
- States why you do it

### **Mission Statement Template**

**Option 1**:
*"To [primary action] by [key approach], empowering [target audience] to [outcome]."*

**Option 2**:
*"We [what we do] for [whom] by [how], enabling them to [outcome]."*

**Option 3**:
*"[Company name] exists to [purpose] by [method], helping [who] achieve [result]."*

### **Mission Builder**

#### **Step 1: Purpose** (10 min)
Define why you exist:

**We exist to:**
- [Primary purpose]
- [Value created]
- [Problem solved]

#### **Step 2: What You Do** (10 min)
Describe what you do:

**Our work includes:**
- [Activity 1]
- [Activity 2]
- [Activity 3]

**Summary**: [One sentence about what you do]

#### **Step 3: Who You Serve** (10 min)
Define your target audience:

**We serve:**
- [Primary audience]: [How you serve them]
- [Secondary audience]: [How you serve them]

**Summary**: [One sentence about who you serve]

#### **Step 4: How You Do It** (10 min)
Describe your unique approach:

**We achieve this through:**
- [Method 1]: [How]
- [Method 2]: [How]
- [Method 3]: [How]

**Summary**: [One sentence about your approach]

#### **Step 5: Draft** (15 min)
Combine elements into mission statement:

**Draft 1**: "[Mission statement]"
**Draft 2**: "[Mission statement]"
**Draft 3**: "[Mission statement]"

#### **Step 6: Refine** (15 min)
Select and refine:

### **Example 1: Technology Company**

**Vision**:
*"To democratize access to AI technology, making advanced intelligence available to everyone, everywhere."*

**Mission**:
*"We build AI tools that empower businesses and individuals to solve complex problems, enabling innovation and growth."*

2. **Accessibility**: We make advanced technology available to all
3. **Excellence**: We deliver high-quality solutions
4. **Impact**: We focus on meaningful results

**Vision**:
*"To become the most trusted partner for small businesses, helping them achieve sustainable growth and long-term success."*

**Mission**:
*"We provide consulting services that help small businesses overcome challenges, optimize operations, and achieve their goals."*

**Values**:
1. **Trust**: We build lasting relationships
2. **Results**: We deliver measurable outcomes
3. **Simplicity**: We make complex things simple
4. **Service**: We put clients first

*"To revolutionize the way people work by creating tools that make productivity simple, beautiful, and joyful."*

**Mission**:
*"We design and build software products that help teams collaborate effectively, work smarter, and achieve more together."*

**Values**:
1. **Design**: We create beautiful, intuitive experiences
2. **Simplicity**: We believe less is more
3. **Joy**: We make work enjoyable
4. **Empowerment**: We give people tools to succeed
