## Risk Assessment Framework

### Risk Categories

#### Technology Risks
- **System Failures:** Hardware, software, network failures
- **Cyber Attacks:** Malware, ransomware, data breaches
- **Data Loss:** Corruption, deletion, unauthorized access
- **Performance Issues:** Slow response, capacity problems
- **Integration Failures:** Third-party service failures

#### Business Risks
- **Revenue Loss:** Service outages, customer churn
- **Reputation Damage:** Negative publicity, customer complaints
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Legal violations, fines
- **Competitive Disadvantage:** Market share loss
- **Operational Disruption:** Process failures, staff issues

#### External Risks
- **Natural Disasters:** Earthquakes, floods, fires
- **Pandemic:** Health crises, remote work challenges
- **Economic Downturn:** Market conditions, customer spending
- **Regulatory Changes:** New laws, compliance requirements
- **Vendor Failures:** Third-party service disruptions

#### Internal Risks
- **Key Personnel Loss:** Resignation, illness, death
- **Knowledge Loss:** Institutional knowledge, expertise
- **Process Failures:** Inadequate procedures, training
- **Financial Issues:** Cash flow, funding, costs
- **Management Issues:** Leadership, decision-making

## Risk Assessment Matrix

### Risk Scoring Methodology

#### Probability Assessment
- **Very High (5):** >80% probability
- **High (4):** 60-80% probability
- **Medium (3):** 40-60% probability
- **Low (2):** 20-40% probability
- **Very Low (1):** <20% probability

#### Impact Assessment
- **Critical (5):** >$1,000,000 impact
- **High (4):** $500,000 - $1,000,000 impact
- **Medium (3):** $100,000 - $500,000 impact
- **Low (2):** $25,000 - $100,000 impact
- **Very Low (1):** <$25,000 impact

#### Risk Score Calculation
**Risk Score = Probability Ã— Impact**

### Risk Rating Levels
- **Critical (20-25):** Immediate action required
- **High (15-19):** Action required within 30 days
- **Medium (10-14):** Action required within 90 days
- **Low (5-9):** Monitor and review
- **Very Low (1-4):** Accept and monitor
