### Prompt 1: Geographic Market Analysis

**Use Case**: Initial market research and geographic opportunity identification  
**Complexity**: ‚≠ê Beginner  
**Time to Complete**: 15-30 minutes  
**Best For**: First-time international expansion planning

**Prompt**:
```
Can you propose potential geographic areas of interest for my {PRODUCT/SERVICE} taking into account its distinct characteristics and target demographic? Which localizations prospects exist in these areas, and how should I take into consideration cultural and idiomatic variances as well as subtle nuances in the language to guarantee a successful localization?
```

**Variables to Replace**:
- `{PRODUCT/SERVICE}`: Your specific product or service name (be descriptive: "AI-powered CRM for SMBs", "B2C fashion e-commerce platform", etc.)

**Expected Output**:
- Ranked list of 5-10 potential geographic markets with rationale
- Localization opportunities per market (language, cultural, regulatory)
- Cultural and linguistic considerations with specific examples
- Language nuances and adaptation strategies
- Market size estimates and growth potential
- Competitive landscape overview

**Pro Tips**:
- Add your current market: "Currently successful in {CURRENT_REGION}"
- Specify budget: "with a budget of {BUDGET} for initial expansion"
- Include constraints: "excluding {REGIONS} due to {REASON}"

### Prompt 2: Regional Adaptation Strategy

**Use Case**: Deep dive into a specific region's localization needs  
**Complexity**: ‚≠ê‚≠ê Intermediate  
**Time to Complete**: 30-45 minutes  
**Best For**: Detailed planning for a specific target market

**Prompt**:
```
I am contemplating the idea of adapting my {PRODUCT/SERVICE} to the specific needs and preferences of {REGION}. I would greatly appreciate your inputs on the local {DEMOGRAPHICS/LANGUAGE} dynamics and their potential impact on my localization approach. What strategies can I implement to ensure that my {CONTENT/ADVERTISING} effectively connects with the target audience in this region?
```

**Variables to Replace**:
- `{PRODUCT/SERVICE}`: Your specific product or service name with key features
- `{REGION}`: Target region or country (be specific: "Japan", "Latin America", "DACH region")
- `{DEMOGRAPHICS/LANGUAGE}`: Specific demographic or language focus (e.g., "millennial demographics aged 25-40", "Spanish language variants", "urban professionals in tech")
- `{CONTENT/ADVERTISING}`: Type of content or advertising material (e.g., "social media campaigns", "website content", "email marketing", "product descriptions")

**Expected Output**:
- Detailed regional demographic and language insights with data points
- Prioritized localization strategy recommendations (high/medium/low priority)
- Content adaptation guidelines with before/after examples
- Cultural connection strategies with specific tactics
- Regulatory and compliance considerations
- Budget estimates for localization efforts
- Timeline recommendations

**Pro Tips**:
- Specify your current content: "My current {CONTENT} focuses on {THEME}"
- Include success metrics: "aiming for {METRIC} in {TIMEFRAME}"
- Mention competitors: "similar to how {COMPETITOR} approaches this market"

### Prompt 3: Consumer Behavior & UX Adaptation

**Use Case**: Understanding local consumer behavior and adapting user experience  
**Complexity**: ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê Advanced  
**Time to Complete**: 45-60 minutes  
**Best For**: Product teams preparing for market entry

**Prompt**:
```
How can I enhance the success of my {PRODUCT/SERVICE} in {REGION} by considering the {CONSUMER BEHAVIOR/BUYING HABITS} and {LANGUAGE USE/LINGUISTIC PATTERNS} specific to the region? What are some recommended strategies for adapting my {UI/UX/CONTENT/BRANDING} to the local market, and what obstacles should I anticipate?
```

**Variables to Replace**:
- `{PRODUCT/SERVICE}`: Your specific product or service name with platform type (web, mobile, desktop)
- `{REGION}`: Target region or country
- `{CONSUMER BEHAVIOR/BUYING HABITS}`: Specific behavior patterns (e.g., "mobile-first purchasing via apps", "seasonal buying patterns during {SEASON}", "preference for {PAYMENT_METHOD}")
- `{LANGUAGE USE/LINGUISTIC PATTERNS}`: Language considerations (e.g., "formal vs informal address in {LANGUAGE}", "regional dialects in {REGION}", "technical terminology preferences in {INDUSTRY}")
- `{UI/UX/CONTENT/BRANDING}`: What aspect to adapt (choose one or multiple: UI, UX, content, branding, pricing, features)

**Expected Output**:
- Consumer behavior insights with supporting data and trends
- Linguistic pattern analysis with examples and recommendations
- Prioritized adaptation strategies for specified areas (quick wins vs. long-term)
- Potential challenges ranked by severity with mitigation strategies
- A/B testing recommendations for validation
- User journey mapping for local market
- Competitive analysis of how others adapted

**Pro Tips**:
- Include user research: "Based on interviews with {NUMBER} users in {REGION}"
- Specify platform: "for {PLATFORM} (iOS/Android/Web)"
- Mention constraints: "with limited budget of {AMOUNT} and {TIMELINE}"

### Prompt 5: Comprehensive Localization Strategy

**Use Case**: Creating an end-to-end localization plan  
**Complexity**: ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê Advanced  
**Time to Complete**: 60-90 minutes  
**Best For**: Strategic planning for multi-market expansion

**Prompt**:

**Variables to Replace**:
- `{PRODUCT/SERVICE}`: Your specific product or service name
- `{LOCALIZATION PLAN/STRATEGY}`: Type of plan needed (e.g., "multi-market localization plan for {NUMBER} countries", "phased localization strategy over {TIMEFRAME}", "agile localization framework")
- `{TRANSLATION/MARKETING/SEO}`: Specific techniques to include (choose one or multiple: translation, transcreation, marketing localization, SEO localization, technical documentation)

**Expected Output**:
- Comprehensive localization framework with phases and milestones

- Cultural, linguistic, and regional adaptation guidelines with checklists
- Resource allocation and team structure recommendations
- Technology stack and tool recommendations

- KPIs and success metrics for measuring localization effectiveness
- Risk management and contingency plans

**Pro Tips**:
- Specify scope: "for {NUMBER} markets: {MARKET1}, {MARKET2}, {MARKET3}"
- Include budget: "with a localization budget of {BUDGET}"
- Set timeline: "to launch in {TIMEFRAME}"
- Mention team: "with a team of {SIZE} people including {ROLES}"

## üéØ Usage Guidelines

### When to Use Each Prompt

| Prompt | Stage | Use When | Next Steps |
|--------|-------|----------|------------|
| **Prompt 1** | Discovery | Starting international expansion, need market identification | ‚Üí Prompt 2 or 4 |
| **Prompt 2** | Planning | Selected a target market, need detailed strategy | ‚Üí Prompt 3 |
| **Prompt 3** | Product Development | Ready to adapt product/UX for market entry | ‚Üí Implementation |
| **Prompt 4** | Scaling | Successful in one market, want to expand | ‚Üí Prompt 5 |
| **Prompt 5** | Strategic Planning | Multi-market expansion, need comprehensive plan | ‚Üí Execution |

### Workflow Recommendations

#### For First-Time International Expansion:
1. Start with **Prompt 1** to identify potential markets
2. Use **Prompt 2** for top 2-3 markets to compare strategies
3. Use **Prompt 3** for the selected market to plan product adaptation
4. Consider **Prompt 5** if planning multiple markets simultaneously

#### For Expanding from Success:
1. Start with **Prompt 4** to identify expansion opportunities
2. Use **Prompt 2** for each target market
3. Use **Prompt 3** for UX/product adaptation
4. Use **Prompt 5** to create unified strategy

### Best Practices & Optimization Tips

#### Prompt Optimization:
- ‚úÖ **Be Specific**: Instead of "SaaS product", use "B2B project management SaaS for teams of 10-50"
- ‚úÖ **Add Context**: Include current metrics, constraints, and goals
- ‚úÖ **Specify Output Format**: Add "Format the response as a structured plan with priorities"
- ‚úÖ **Request Examples**: Add "Include 2-3 real-world examples for each recommendation"
- ‚úÖ **Set Scope**: Clarify budget, timeline, and team size constraints

#### Variable Replacement:
- **Product/Service**: Include type, target audience, key features, and current market
- **Region**: Be specific (country > region > continent) and mention any sub-regions
- **Demographics**: Include age range, income level, tech-savviness, and behavior patterns
- **Metrics**: Include current performance, growth rates, and target goals

#### Iterative Refinement:
1. **First Pass**: Use prompt as-is to get broad insights
2. **Second Pass**: Add specific constraints and refine based on initial output
3. **Third Pass**: Deep dive into top 2-3 recommendations with follow-up questions
4. **Validation**: Cross-reference with market research and expert opinions
