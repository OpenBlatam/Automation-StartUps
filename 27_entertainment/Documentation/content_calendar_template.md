## üé® **Content Format Guidelines**

### Blog Posts
- **Length:** 1,500-3,000 words
- **Structure:** Clear headings, bullet points, visual breaks
- **SEO:** Keyword optimization, meta descriptions
- **Engagement:** CTAs, social sharing, comments

### Social Media Posts
- **LinkedIn:** 1,300 characters, professional tone
- **Twitter:** 280 characters, engaging, hashtags
- **Facebook:** 1,000 characters, conversational tone
- **Instagram:** Visual focus, engaging captions

### Video Content
- **Tutorials:** 5-15 minutes, clear structure
- **Interviews:** 15-30 minutes, engaging questions
- **Quick Tips:** 1-3 minutes, actionable advice
- **Webinars:** 60-90 minutes, interactive elements

### Infographics
- **Size:** 1200x630px for social sharing
- **Content:** Key statistics, visual hierarchy
- **Branding:** Consistent colors, fonts, logo
- **Accessibility:** Clear text, high contrast

## üìä **Content Analytics Dashboard**

### Key Performance Indicators
- **Content Views:** Page views, unique visitors
- **Engagement Rate:** Likes, comments, shares
- **Time on Page:** Average reading time
- **Bounce Rate:** Single-page sessions
- **Conversion Rate:** CTA clicks, form submissions

### Social Media Metrics
- **Reach:** Total audience reached
- **Impressions:** Total views of content
- **Engagement:** Likes, comments, shares, saves
- **Click-through Rate:** Clicks to website
- **Follower Growth:** New followers gained

### Email Marketing Metrics
- **Open Rate:** Percentage of emails opened
- **Click Rate:** Percentage of clicks on links
- **Unsubscribe Rate:** Percentage of unsubscribes
- **Conversion Rate:** Actions taken from emails
- **List Growth:** New subscribers added

### Content Performance Rankings
- **Top Performing Content:** By views, engagement, conversions
- **Most Shared Content:** Social media performance
- **Highest Converting Content:** Lead generation performance
- **Most Engaging Content:** Comments, time on page
- **Trending Content:** Recent performance spikes
