## Customization Guidelines

### For Different Roles
- **Technical Roles**: Extended technical training, coding bootcamps, system architecture deep-dives, AI/ML frameworks
- **Sales Roles**: Product demos, customer interaction training, CRM mastery, AI ROI demonstrations
- **Marketing Roles**: Brand guidelines, campaign tools, analytics platforms, AI content generation
- **Support Roles**: Customer service training, product knowledge, escalation procedures, AI troubleshooting
- **Content Creators**: AI writing tools, video production, course design, student engagement
- **Data Scientists**: Model development, data analysis, AI ethics, MLOps practices

### For Different Experience Levels
- **Entry Level**: More structured training, closer supervision, detailed documentation, AI fundamentals
- **Mid-Level**: Faster pace, more independence, cross-functional exposure, AI specialization
- **Senior Level**: Strategic focus, leadership opportunities, mentoring responsibilities, AI innovation
- **AI Experts**: Research focus, technical leadership, innovation projects, industry thought leadership

### For Remote vs. On-site
- **Remote**: Enhanced virtual onboarding, digital collaboration tools, regular video check-ins, AI tool demos
- **Hybrid**: Flexible schedule coordination, both virtual and in-person elements, AI lab access
- **On-site**: Office culture immersion, in-person relationship building, physical workspace optimization, AI hardware access
