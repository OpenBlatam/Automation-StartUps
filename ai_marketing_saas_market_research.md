# Market Research: AI Marketing SaaS Platform

## Executive Summary

The AI marketing SaaS market is experiencing rapid growth as businesses seek to automate and optimize their marketing operations. This analysis examines customer preferences, behaviors, and competitive landscape for AI-powered marketing platforms, providing strategic insights for market entry and positioning.

### Market Size & Growth
- **Global AI Marketing Market**: $15.8 billion (2023) → $107.5 billion (2030)
- **CAGR**: 35.2% (2023-2030)
- **Target Segments**:

  - Customer analytics & insights: $3.8B
