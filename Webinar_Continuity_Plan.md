**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the business continuity procedures specifically for our AI course webinar operations, ensuring uninterrupted delivery of educational content to our students.

## Webinar Operations Overview

### Current Infrastructure
- **Primary Platform:** Zoom Pro
- **Backup Platform:** Microsoft Teams
- **Recording:** Zoom Cloud + Local Recording
- **Streaming:** YouTube Live (backup)
- **Registration:** Eventbrite + Custom System

### Key Stakeholders
- **Webinar Host:** Primary instructor
- **Technical Support:** IT team member
- **Marketing:** Registration and promotion
- **Students:** 500+ registered participants

---

## Disruption Scenarios & Response

### Scenario 1: Primary Platform Failure (Zoom Outage)

**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High

#### Immediate Response (0-15 minutes)
- **Trigger:** Zoom service unavailable or connection issues
- **Actions:**
  1. Check Zoom status page
  2. Notify all registered participants via email
  3. Activate backup platform (Teams)
  4. Update webinar links

#### Communication Protocol:
```
Subject: URGENT - Webinar Platform Change

Dear [Student Name],

Due to technical difficulties with our primary platform, we're switching to Microsoft Teams for today's webinar.

New link: [Teams Link]
Meeting ID: [ID]
Password: [Password]

Please join 5 minutes early to test your connection.

We apologize for any inconvenience.

Best regards,
[Instructor Name]
```

#### Recovery Timeline: 15-30 minutes
**Leadership:** Webinar Host + Technical Support

---

### Scenario 2: Internet Connectivity Issues

**Probability:** High | **Impact:** Critical

#### Immediate Response (0-10 minutes)
- **Actions:**
  1. Switch to mobile hotspot
  2. Use backup internet connection
  3. Move to alternative location if needed
  4. Notify participants of potential delays

#### Backup Locations:
- **Primary:** Office with fiber internet
- **Secondary:** Co-working space
- **Tertiary:** Home office with backup connection

#### Recovery Timeline: 10-30 minutes
**Leadership:** Webinar Host

---

### Scenario 3: Host Unavailability (Illness/Emergency)

**Probability:** Low | **Impact:** Critical

#### Immediate Response (0-30 minutes)
- **Actions:**
  1. Activate backup instructor
  2. Use pre-recorded content
  3. Reschedule with 24-hour notice
  4. Provide alternative learning materials

#### Backup Instructors:
- **Primary Backup:** [Name] - [Contact]
- **Secondary Backup:** [Name] - [Contact]
- **Pre-recorded Content:** Available for all sessions

#### Communication Template:
```
Subject: Webinar Update - Host Change

Dear [Student Name],

Due to unforeseen circumstances, [Original Host] is unable to lead today's webinar.

[Backup Host] will be conducting the session instead.
Time remains: [Original Time]
Platform: [Platform]

All content and Q&A will proceed as planned.

Thank you for your understanding.

Best regards,
[Company Name] Team
```

---

### Scenario 4: Technical Equipment Failure

**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium

#### Equipment Backup Plan:
- **Primary Setup:** Professional camera + microphone
- **Backup Setup:** Laptop camera + headset
- **Emergency Setup:** Mobile device

#### Recovery Timeline: 5-15 minutes
**Leadership:** Technical Support
