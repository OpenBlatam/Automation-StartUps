## üìã Regulatory Compliance

### **Data Protection & Privacy**

#### **GDPR Compliance (European Union)**
**Requirements**:
- **Lawful Basis**: Identify lawful basis for data processing
- **Data Minimization**: Collect only necessary data
- **Purpose Limitation**: Use data only for stated purposes
- **Data Accuracy**: Ensure data accuracy and currency
- **Storage Limitation**: Retain data only as long as necessary
- **Security**: Implement appropriate security measures

**Implementation**:
- **Privacy by Design**: Integrate privacy into all systems
- **Data Protection Impact Assessments**: Conduct DPIAs for high-risk processing
- **Data Processing Agreements**: Execute DPAs with all processors
- **Consent Management**: Implement robust consent management
- **Data Subject Rights**: Enable exercise of data subject rights
- **Breach Notification**: Implement breach notification procedures

#### **CCPA Compliance (California)**
**Requirements**:
- **Notice at Collection**: Provide notice when collecting personal information
- **Right to Know**: Provide information about data collection and use
- **Right to Delete**: Honor requests to delete personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Honor opt-out requests for sale of personal information
- **Non-Discrimination**: Not discriminate against consumers who exercise rights
- **Authorized Agent**: Allow authorized agents to make requests

**Implementation**:
- **Privacy Policy**: Maintain comprehensive privacy policy
- **Consumer Rights Portal**: Provide easy-to-use rights portal
- **Data Inventory**: Maintain inventory of personal information
- **Vendor Management**: Ensure vendor compliance with CCPA
- **Employee Training**: Train employees on CCPA requirements
- **Record Keeping**: Maintain records of consumer requests

#### **Other Privacy Regulations**
- **PIPEDA (Canada)**: Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act
- **LGPD (Brazil)**: Lei Geral de Prote√ß√£o de Dados
