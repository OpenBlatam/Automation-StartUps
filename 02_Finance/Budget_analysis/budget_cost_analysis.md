## Cost-Benefit Analysis

### Revenue Protection

#### Potential Revenue Loss Without BCP
- **Service Outage (1 day):** $50,000 - $100,000
- **Service Outage (1 week):** $350,000 - $700,000
- **Service Outage (1 month):** $1,500,000 - $3,000,000
- **Data Breach:** $500,000 - $2,000,000
- **Regulatory Fines:** $100,000 - $1,000,000

#### Revenue Protection with BCP
- **Service Outage (1 day):** $5,000 - $10,000 (90% reduction)
- **Service Outage (1 week):** $35,000 - $70,000 (90% reduction)
- **Service Outage (1 month):** $150,000 - $300,000 (90% reduction)
- **Data Breach:** $50,000 - $200,000 (90% reduction)
- **Regulatory Fines:** $10,000 - $100,000 (90% reduction)

- **Business Interruption Insurance:** 20-30% premium reduction
- **Cyber Liability Insurance:** 15-25% premium reduction

#### Operational Efficiency
- **Reduced Downtime:** 90% reduction in downtime costs
- **Faster Recovery:** 80% faster recovery times
- **Improved Reliability:** 99.9% uptime target
- **Customer Retention:** 95% customer retention during outages

### Return on Investment (ROI)
