## üìä **Content Structure Optimization**

### Title Optimization
**Formula:** [Number] + [Adjective] + [Topic] + [Benefit] + [Year/Timeframe]

**Examples:**
- "10 Essential AI Skills That Will Future-Proof Your Career in 2024"
- "5 AI Marketing Tools That Increased Our ROI by 300%"
- "The Complete AI Learning Roadmap: From Beginner to Expert in 6 Months"

### Headline Hierarchy
1. **H1:** Main title (includes primary keyword)
2. **H2:** Section headers (include secondary keywords)
3. **H3:** Subsection headers (include long-tail keywords)
4. **H4:** Supporting points (include semantic keywords)

### Content Length Optimization
- **Minimum:** 1,500 words for comprehensive coverage
- **Optimal:** 2,500-3,500 words for high-ranking content
- **Maximum:** 4,000 words for in-depth guides
