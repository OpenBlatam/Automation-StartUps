## üèóÔ∏è Agile Frameworks & Methodologies

### Agile Frameworks Overview

#### 1. Scrum Framework
- **Scrum Roles**: Product Owner, Scrum Master, Development Team
- **Scrum Events**: Sprint Planning, Daily Scrum, Sprint Review, Retrospective
- **Scrum Artifacts**: Product Backlog, Sprint Backlog, Increment
- **Scrum Values**: Commitment, Courage, Focus, Openness, Respect

#### 2. Kanban Framework
- **Kanban Principles**: Visualize, Limit Work in Progress, Manage Flow
- **Kanban Board**: Visual workflow management
- **Kanban Metrics**: Lead Time, Cycle Time, Throughput
- **Continuous Improvement**: Kaizen and continuous optimization

#### 3. Lean Agile
- **Lean Principles**: Eliminate Waste, Amplify Learning, Decide as Late as Possible
- **Value Stream Mapping**: Map and optimize value streams
- **Continuous Flow**: Optimize continuous flow of value
- **Pull Systems**: Implement pull-based systems

#### 4. SAFe (Scaled Agile Framework)
- **SAFe Levels**: Team, Program, Large Solution, Portfolio
- **SAFe Roles**: Product Manager, Release Train Engineer, System Architect
- **SAFe Events**: PI Planning, System Demo, Inspect & Adapt
- **SAFe Artifacts**: Program Backlog, Features, User Stories

### Methodology Selection

#### 1. Scrum Selection Criteria
- **Project Type**: Software development, product development
- **Team Size**: 3-9 team members
- **Requirements**: Changing requirements and priorities
- **Timeline**: Fixed time-boxed iterations

#### 2. Kanban Selection Criteria
- **Process Type**: Support, maintenance, operations
- **Team Size**: Any size team
- **Requirements**: Continuous flow of work
- **Timeline**: Continuous delivery

#### 3. SAFe Selection Criteria
- **Organization Size**: Large organizations (100+ people)
- **Complexity**: Complex products and systems
- **Coordination**: Need for coordination across teams
- **Scale**: Multiple teams and programs
