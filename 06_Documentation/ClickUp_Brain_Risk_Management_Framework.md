# ClickUp Brain Risk Management Framework

## Executive Summary

The ClickUp Brain Risk Management Framework provides a comprehensive approach to risk management, enabling organizations to identify, assess, mitigate, and monitor risks across all business operations. This framework integrates risk identification, risk assessment, risk mitigation, risk monitoring, and risk governance to create a robust risk management system that protects organizational value and ensures business continuity.
