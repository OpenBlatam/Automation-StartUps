#### 2. Process Assessment
- **Volume Analysis**: Analyze process volume and frequency

- **Error Analysis**: Identify common errors and failure points
- **Cost Analysis**: Calculate current process costs

#### 3. Automation Potential
- **Automation Readiness**: Assess process automation readiness
- **ROI Calculation**: Calculate potential automation ROI
- **Risk Assessment**: Identify automation risks and challenges
- **Priority Ranking**: Rank processes by automation priority

### Process Selection Criteria

#### 1. High-Value Processes
- **High Volume**: Processes with high transaction volume
- **High Cost**: Processes with significant manual costs
- **High Error Rate**: Processes prone to errors and rework
- **High Impact**: Processes with significant business impact

#### 2. Automation-Friendly Processes
- **Rule-Based**: Processes with clear, consistent rules
- **Structured Data**: Processes using structured data
- **Repetitive Tasks**: Processes with repetitive, routine tasks
- **System Integration**: Processes involving multiple systems

#### 3. Quick Win Opportunities
- **Low Complexity**: Simple processes with low automation complexity
- **High ROI**: Processes with high return on investment
- **Low Risk**: Processes with low implementation risk
- **High Visibility**: Processes with high business visibility
