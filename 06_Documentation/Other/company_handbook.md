## üè¢ Workplace Policies

### Work Schedule & Hours

#### **Standard Work Hours**
- **Full-time**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (local time)
- **Flexible hours**: 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM (with manager approval)
- **Remote work**: Available with manager approval and business justification
- **Overtime**: Must be pre-approved by manager

#### **Time Tracking**
- All employees must accurately record work hours
- Use company-approved time tracking system
- Report time daily or weekly as required
- Overtime must be documented and approved

### Remote Work Policy

#### **Eligibility**
- Based on role requirements and manager approval
- Must have suitable home office setup
- Reliable internet connection required
- Ability to maintain productivity and communication

#### **Requirements**
- Dedicated workspace free from distractions
- Company-provided equipment and software
- Regular communication with team and manager
- Participation in virtual meetings and collaboration

#### **Expectations**
- Maintain same productivity standards as office work
- Be available during core business hours
- Attend required in-person meetings and events
- Follow all company policies and procedures

### Attendance & Punctuality

#### **Expectations**
- Arrive on time for work and meetings
- Notify manager of late arrivals or absences
- Plan personal appointments outside work hours when possible
- Maintain professional communication about schedule changes

#### **Absence Reporting**
- Notify manager as soon as possible
- Use company absence reporting system
- Provide appropriate documentation for extended absences
- Follow up on return to work

## üö® Safety & Emergency Procedures

### Workplace Safety
- **Safety First**: Prioritize safety in all work activities
- **Report Hazards**: Immediately report unsafe conditions or practices
- **Follow Procedures**: Adhere to all safety protocols and procedures
- **Emergency Preparedness**: Know emergency procedures and evacuation routes

### Emergency Procedures

#### **Medical Emergencies**
- Call 911 for life-threatening emergencies
- Notify security and management immediately
- Provide first aid if trained and safe to do so
- Document incident and follow up as required

#### **Fire Emergencies**
- Pull fire alarm and evacuate immediately
- Use designated evacuation routes
- Assemble at designated meeting points
- Do not re-enter building until cleared by authorities

#### **Security Incidents**
- Report suspicious activity immediately
- Follow security protocols and procedures
- Cooperate with security personnel
- Document incidents and provide witness statements

### Remote Work Safety
- **Home Office Safety**: Maintain safe and ergonomic workspace
- **Data Security**: Protect company information and systems
- **Communication**: Maintain regular contact with team and manager
- **Emergency Contacts**: Provide current contact information
