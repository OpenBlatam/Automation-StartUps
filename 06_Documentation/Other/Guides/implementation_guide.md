## ðŸš¨ Risk Management & Mitigation

### **Technical Risks**
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|-------------|--------|-------------------|
| System Downtime | Medium | High | Backup systems, offline mode |
| Data Loss | Low | High | Regular backups, redundancy |
| Security Breach | Low | High | Security audits, monitoring |
| Integration Issues | Medium | Medium | Thorough testing, fallback plans |

### **Process Risks**
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|-------------|--------|-------------------|
| Low Adoption | Medium | High | Training, incentives, support |
| Supervisor Resistance | Medium | Medium | Training, clear benefits |
| Content Outdated | High | Medium | Regular review, updates |
| Resource Constraints | Medium | Medium | Phased rollout, prioritization |

### **Contingency Plans**
- [ ] **System Failure**: Offline mode with manual tracking
- [ ] **Low Adoption**: Additional training and incentives
- [ ] **Resource Issues**: Prioritize critical roles first
- [ ] **Quality Concerns**: Immediate feedback and adjustment
