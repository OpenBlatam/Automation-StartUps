## üéì AI Education & Training Companies

### Example 1: Coursera-Style AI Course Platform

#### Company Profile
- **Industry**: AI Education & Online Learning
- **Size**: 500+ employees
- **Focus**: AI courses, certifications, and skill development
- **Challenge**: Onboarding content creators and technical instructors

#### Onboarding Process
```
# AI Course Platform Onboarding

## Pre-Boarding (Week -2 to -1)
- [ ] Send AI education industry overview
- [ ] Provide access to existing courses and content
- [ ] Share student success stories and testimonials
- [ ] Set up course creation tools and LMS access
- [ ] Assign mentor from content creation team

## Week 1: Platform Mastery
- [ ] Company mission: Democratizing AI education
- [ ] Course platform walkthrough and training
- [ ] Student journey and learning experience
- [ ] Content creation tools and workflows
- [ ] Video production and editing training
- [ ] Assessment design and implementation
- [ ] Student engagement strategies
- [ ] AI ethics and responsible education

## Week 2-3: Content Creation
- [ ] AI fundamentals and current trends
- [ ] Course creation platform training
- [ ] Video production and editing tools
- [ ] Learning management system navigation
- [ ] Student engagement strategies
- [ ] Assessment and feedback systems
- [ ] AI prompt engineering and best practices
- [ ] Interactive AI tool demonstrations

## 30-Day Project: Create Mini-Course
- **Objective**: Create a 30-minute course on AI fundamentals
- **Deliverables**: Course outline, video content, assessments
- **Success Criteria**: Course completion rate >80%, student satisfaction >4.5/5
```

#### Key Success Factors
- **Content-First Approach**: Focus on creating high-quality educational content
- **Student-Centric Design**: Understanding learner needs and preferences
- **AI Ethics Integration**: Responsible AI education and development
- **Technical Proficiency**: Mastery of course creation tools and platforms

#### Results
- **Time to Productivity**: 35 days (vs. 60 days industry average)
- **90-Day Retention**: 92%
- **Student Satisfaction**: 4.7/5.0
- **Course Quality**: 95% pass rate on quality reviews
