### **Neuromarketing Principles:**
- **FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)** - Creates urgency and scarcity
- **Social Proof** - Leverages herd mentality and authority
- **Loss Aversion** - Emphasizes what users will lose without action
- **Reciprocity** - Creates sense of obligation and value
- **Authority Bias** - Positions as expert and trustworthy source

### **Behavioral Psychology:**
- **Cognitive Dissonance** - Creates mental tension that needs resolution
- **Confirmation Bias** - Appeals to existing beliefs and desires
- **Anchoring Effect** - Sets high-value expectations
- **Bandwagon Effect** - Creates social pressure to join
- **Scarcity Principle** - Makes content appear more valuable
