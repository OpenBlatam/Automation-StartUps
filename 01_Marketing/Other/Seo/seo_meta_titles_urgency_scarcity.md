## üé® **Urgency & Scarcity Strategies**

### **Time-Based Urgency:**
- **Specific deadlines** - Exact times and dates
- **Countdown timers** - Visual time pressure
- **Limited duration** - Short time windows
- **Expiration dates** - Clear end times
- **Seasonal limits** - Holiday and event-based urgency

### **Quantity-Based Scarcity:**
- **Limited spots** - Specific number restrictions
- **Remaining count** - Show how many left
- **Exclusive access** - VIP and premium restrictions
- **Beta limitations** - Early access restrictions
- **Capacity limits** - System and resource constraints

### **Value-Based Urgency:**
- **Price increases** - Upcoming cost changes
- **Discount expiration** - Sale end dates
- **Bonus removal** - Limited-time extras
- **Feature changes** - Upcoming modifications
- **Access restrictions** - Future limitations
