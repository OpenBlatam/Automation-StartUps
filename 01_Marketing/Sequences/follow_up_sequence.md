## Follow-Up Sequence Performance Expectations

### Engagement Metrics
- **Open Rate:** 35-45% (high engagement from re-engaged subscribers)
- **Click-Through Rate:** 18-25% (strong interest in new content)
- **Conversion Rate:** 8-12% (course enrollment and tool usage)
- **Community Join Rate:** 15-20% (social engagement)

### Revenue Metrics
- **Course Enrollment:** 8-12% of re-engaged subscribers
- **Tool Usage:** 25-30% of re-engaged subscribers
- **Community Engagement:** 15-20% of re-engaged subscribers
- **Average Revenue per Re-engaged Subscriber:** $150-300

## Segmentation for Follow-Up Sequence

### High-Value Re-engaged Subscribers
- **Focus:** Advanced features and premium content

### Long-Time Re-engaged Subscribers

## Automation Triggers

### Email 1: "Welcome Back"
**Trigger:** Opens any win-back email
**Delay:** 1 hour
**Condition:** Hasn't opened follow-up emails

### Email 2: "Quick Win"
**Trigger:** Opens "Welcome Back" email
**Delay:** 2 days
**Condition:** Hasn't clicked AI tool link

### Email 3: "Success Story"
**Trigger:** Clicks AI tool link OR 7 days since re-engagement
**Delay:** 7 days
**Condition:** Hasn't enrolled in course

### Email 4: "Exclusive Access"
**Trigger:** Opens "Success Story" email
**Delay:** 7 days
**Condition:** Hasn't accessed beta features

### Email 5: "Community Invitation"
**Trigger:** Clicks beta access link OR 21 days since re-engagement
**Delay:** 21 days
**Condition:** Hasn't joined community

### Email 6: "Course Enrollment"
**Trigger:** Joins community OR 28 days since re-engagement
**Delay:** 28 days
**Condition:** Hasn't enrolled in course

## Success Metrics and Optimization

### Key Performance Indicators
1. **Re-engagement Rate:** % of win-back subscribers who engage with follow-up
2. **Tool Adoption Rate:** % who use AI bulk document generator
3. **Community Participation:** % who join private community
4. **Course Conversion:** % who enroll in AI Mastery Course
5. **Revenue Recovery:** Total revenue from re-engaged subscribers

### Optimization Strategies
1. **A/B Test Subject Lines:** Test different approaches for each email
2. **Personalize Content:** Use subscriber data to customize messaging
3. **Timing Optimization:** Test different send times and delays
4. **Content Variation:** Test different value propositions and offers
5. **Segmentation Refinement:** Continuously improve segmentation rules

### Expected Overall Performance
- **Re-engagement Rate:** 60-70% of win-back subscribers
- **Tool Usage:** 25-30% of re-engaged subscribers
- **Community Join:** 15-20% of re-engaged subscribers
- **Course Enrollment:** 8-12% of re-engaged subscribers
- **Revenue Recovery:** $150-300 per re-engaged subscriber
