## SEO Best Practices Applied

✅ **Focus keyword at the beginning** - All titles start with primary keyword
✅ **Sentiment words included** - Positive emotional triggers in every title
✅ **Power words used** - Proven, Smart, Essential, Ultimate, Secret
✅ **Numbers included** - 3, 5, 7 for increased click-through rates
✅ **Under 55 characters** - All titles optimized for search result display
✅ **Webinar-specific benefits** - Live learning, exclusivity, expert knowledge
✅ **Social proof elements** - Audience numbers and testimonials
✅ **Clear value proposition** - Specific outcomes and benefits

## Recommended Primary Title
**"AI Webinar: 5 Proven Secrets That 10,000+ Marketers Use"**

This title performs best because it:
- Leads with the focus keyword
- Uses a strong power word ("Proven")
- Includes social proof ("10,000+ Marketers")
- Creates credibility and trust
- Promises exclusive knowledge ("Secrets")
- Stays within optimal character count
- Appeals to the target audience (marketers)
