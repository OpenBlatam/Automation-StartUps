## üìä Marketing Analytics & Measurement

### **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**

#### **Awareness Metrics**
- **Brand Awareness**: Brand recognition and recall
- **Website Traffic**: Organic and paid traffic growth
- **Social Media Reach**: Social media impressions and reach
- **Content Engagement**: Content views, shares, comments
- **PR Mentions**: Media mentions and coverage

#### **Acquisition Metrics**
- **Lead Generation**: Number of leads generated
- **Conversion Rates**: Website and campaign conversion rates
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)**: Cost to acquire new customers
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Total cost to acquire customers
- **Trial Sign-ups**: Number of free trial sign-ups

#### **Engagement Metrics**
- **Email Open Rates**: Email marketing performance
- **Social Media Engagement**: Likes, shares, comments
- **Content Consumption**: Time spent on content
- **Feature Adoption**: Usage of platform features
- **User Retention**: User retention rates

#### **Revenue Metrics**
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Monthly revenue growth
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Average customer value
- **Revenue Per User (RPU)**: Revenue per user
- **Churn Rate**: Customer churn rate
- **Expansion Revenue**: Revenue from existing customers

### **Marketing Attribution**

#### **Attribution Models**
- **First-Touch Attribution**: Credit to first marketing touchpoint
- **Last-Touch Attribution**: Credit to last marketing touchpoint
- **Multi-Touch Attribution**: Credit across multiple touchpoints
- **Time-Decay Attribution**: More credit to recent touchpoints
- **Position-Based Attribution**: More credit to first and last touchpoints

#### **Tracking & Analytics**
- **Google Analytics**: Website and campaign tracking
- **Marketing Automation**: Lead tracking and nurturing
- **CRM Integration**: Sales and marketing alignment
- **Attribution Tools**: Advanced attribution analysis
- **Custom Dashboards**: Real-time marketing performance
