# Market Research Integration Guide
## VC Market Intelligence Framework

### Market Research Sources

#### Primary Research
- **Customer Interviews**: Direct customer feedback and validation
- **Expert Networks**: Industry expert consultations
- **Advisory Boards**: Portfolio company advisory insights
- **Conferences**: Industry conference intelligence
- **Surveys**: Market research surveys

#### Secondary Research
- **Industry Reports**: Gartner, Forrester, McKinsey reports
- **Market Data**: CB Insights, PitchBook, Crunchbase
- **Academic Research**: University studies and papers
- **Government Data**: Regulatory and policy information
- **Media Analysis**: Industry publications and news

#### Competitive Intelligence
- **Competitor Analysis**: Direct competitor research
- **Patent Research**: IP landscape analysis
- **Funding Intelligence**: Competitor funding rounds
- **Product Analysis**: Competitor product reviews
- **Market Positioning**: Competitive positioning analysis

### Market Research Framework

#### Market Sizing Analysis
**Total Addressable Market (TAM)**
- Market size calculation methodology
- Growth rate assumptions
- Market segmentation
- Geographic considerations

**Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)**
- Target customer segments
- Geographic limitations
- Product/service constraints
- Market accessibility

**Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)**
- Realistic market share potential
- Competitive landscape
- Market penetration timeline
- Revenue potential

#### Market Timing Assessment
**Market Readiness Factors**
- Technology adoption curve
- Regulatory environment
- Economic conditions
- Customer willingness to pay

**Market Drivers**
- Key market trends
- Adoption catalysts
- Growth accelerators
- Market inflection points

**Market Barriers**
- Adoption barriers
- Regulatory hurdles
- Competitive threats
- Market saturation
