**1. Google Calendar**
- **Best For**: Simple scheduling, team coordination
- **Features**: Shared calendars, reminders, integration

- **Pros**: Easy to use, widely adopted
- **Cons**: Limited content management features

- **Best For**: Visual content planning, team collaboration

- **Cons**: Can become cluttered with many cards

- **Best For**: Project management, team coordination
- **Features**: Tasks, projects, timelines, dependencies
- **Pricing**: Free (basic), $10.99/user/month (premium)
- **Pros**: Powerful project management, good integrations
- **Cons**: Steeper learning curve

**4. Monday.com**
- **Best For**: Team collaboration, workflow management
- **Features**: Customizable boards, automation, integrations
- **Pricing**: $8/user/month (basic)
- **Pros**: Highly customizable, visual
- **Cons**: Can be expensive for large teams

**5. Airtable**
- **Best For**: Database-style content management
- **Features**: Spreadsheet + database, views, automation
- **Pricing**: Free (basic), $10/user/month (plus)
- **Pros**: Flexible, powerful views
- **Cons**: Learning curve for advanced features

- **Features**: Tasks, docs, goals, time tracking
