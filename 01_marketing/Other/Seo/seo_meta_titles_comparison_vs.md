## üé® **Comparison Title Strategies**

### **"vs" Format:**
- **Direct comparison** - Clear head-to-head format
- **Competitive positioning** - Shows superiority
- **Decision support** - Helps users choose
- **Example**: "AI Course vs Traditional: 7 Ways AI Beats Old Methods"

### **"Better Than" Format:**
- **Superiority claim** - Positions as winner
- **Confidence building** - Shows authority
- **Value proposition** - Clear benefit statement
- **Example**: "AI Course Better Than ChatGPT: 5 Proven Strategies"

### **"Beats" Format:**
- **Victory language** - Competitive advantage
- **Action-oriented** - Dynamic and engaging
- **Results-focused** - Outcome-oriented
- **Example**: "AI Course Beats Competitors: 3 Essential Skills That Dominate"

### **"Outperforms" Format:**
- **Performance focus** - Metrics and results
- **Professional tone** - Business-oriented
- **Data-driven** - Evidence-based claims
- **Example**: "AI Course Outperforms Free Training: 7 Smart Tools That Pay Off"
