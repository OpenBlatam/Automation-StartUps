## üìò **Facebook & Instagram**

### **Best Practices**
- Post 3-5 times per week
- Mix of content: 40% educational, 30% promotional, 30% entertaining
- Engage within first hour of posting
- Use Stories daily

### **Content Formats**
- **Feed Posts**: High-quality visuals
- **Stories**: Behind-the-scenes, polls, highlights
- **Reels**: Short-form video (15-90 seconds)
- **Live**: Q&As, tutorials, announcements

### **Optimal Posting Times**
- **Facebook**: 8-10 AM, 12-2 PM, 3-5 PM
- **Instagram**: 11 AM-1 PM, 7-9 PM

### **Hashtag Strategy**
- 5-10 relevant hashtags
- Mix of branded, industry, trending
- Location-based hashtags for local businesses

---

## üíº **LinkedIn**

### **Best Practices**
- Post 2-3 times per week (B2B)
- Professional yet personal tone
- Share industry insights
- Engage with comments thoughtfully

### **Content Formats**
- **Posts**: Professional insights (1,300-2,000 chars)
- **Articles**: Long-form thought leadership
- **Videos**: Native uploads (under 10 mins)
- **Document Posts**: PDFs, presentations

### **Optimal Posting Times**
- Tuesday-Thursday: 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM

### **Engagement Strategy**
- Comment on industry posts
- Share valuable content
- Participate in relevant groups
- Build professional network

---

## üê¶ **Twitter (X)**

### **Best Practices**
- Post multiple times daily (10-20)
- Real-time engagement
- Respond quickly (under 5 mins)
- Use trending topics strategically

### **Content Formats**
- **Tweets**: 280 characters (concise, clear)
- **Threads**: Multiple connected tweets
- **Images**: Graphics, screenshots
- **Videos**: Native uploads (2:20 limit)

### **Optimal Posting Times**
- Weekdays: 8-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM

### **Hashtag Strategy**
- 1-2 relevant hashtags
- Trending hashtags (when relevant)
- Branded hashtags for campaigns

---

## üéµ **TikTok**

### **Best Practices**
- Post 1-2 times daily
- Authentic, creative content
- Jump on trending audio/sounds
- Engage with comments quickly

### **Content Formats**
- **Short Videos**: 15-60 seconds
- **Trending Sounds**: Jump on virality
- **Challenges**: Brand-relevant
- **Tutorials**: Quick how-tos

### **Optimal Posting Times**
- 6-10 AM, 7-9 PM (local time)

### **Strategy**
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Use captions effectively
- Cross-promote on other platforms
- Collaborate with creators
