## Gestión de Riesgos y Issues

### Identificación de Riesgos

#### **Risk Categories**
1. **Technical Risks**: Problemas técnicos y de integración
2. **Business Risks**: Riesgos de negocio y adopción
3. **Operational Risks**: Riesgos operacionales
4. **Security Risks**: Riesgos de seguridad
5. **Compliance Risks**: Riesgos de compliance

#### **Risk Assessment Matrix**
| Risk Level | Probability | Impact | Action Required |
|------------|-------------|--------|-----------------|
| **Critical** | High | High | Immediate action |
| **High** | Medium | High | Urgent action |
| **Medium** | Medium | Medium | Planned action |
| **Low** | Low | Low | Monitor |

### Issue Management Process

#### **Issue Classification**
1. **Critical**: Sistema down, data loss, security breach
2. **High**: Performance issues, integration failures
3. **Medium**: Feature bugs, configuration issues
4. **Low**: Minor bugs, enhancement requests

#### **Issue Resolution Process**
1. **Issue Identification**: Identificación del issue
2. **Classification**: Clasificación del issue
3. **Assignment**: Asignación a equipo apropiado
4. **Investigation**: Investigación del issue
5. **Resolution**: Resolución del issue
6. **Verification**: Verificación de resolución
7. **Documentation**: Documentación del issue

#### **Escalation Matrix**
| Issue Level | Response Time | Resolution Time | Escalation |
|-------------|---------------|-----------------|------------|
| **Critical** | 15 minutes | 2 hours | C-level |
| **High** | 1 hour | 8 hours | Director |
| **Medium** | 4 hours | 24 hours | Manager |
| **Low** | 24 hours | 72 hours | Team Lead |
