## Executive Summary

This document outlines the cybersecurity incident response procedures for detecting, containing, and recovering from security incidents that could impact our business operations.

## Security Incident Classification

### Incident Severity Levels

#### Level 1: Low Severity
- **Definition:** Minor security incidents with limited impact
- **Examples:** Failed login attempts, minor malware detection
- **Impact:** Minimal business impact
- **Response Time:** 24 hours
- **Response Team:** Security Team

#### Level 2: Medium Severity
- **Definition:** Moderate security incidents with some business impact
- **Examples:** Successful phishing attempts, data exposure
- **Impact:** Moderate business impact
- **Response Time:** 4 hours
- **Response Team:** Security Team + IT Manager

#### Level 3: High Severity
- **Definition:** Significant security incidents with major business impact
- **Examples:** Data breach, system compromise, ransomware
- **Impact:** Major business impact
- **Response Time:** 1 hour
- **Response Team:** Full Security Team + Management

#### Level 4: Critical Severity
- **Definition:** Critical security incidents with catastrophic impact
- **Examples:** Massive data breach, complete system compromise
- **Impact:** Catastrophic business impact
- **Response Time:** 15 minutes
- **Response Team:** Full Security Team + Executive Team
