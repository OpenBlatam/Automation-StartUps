## Regulatory Landscape

### Global Regulations

#### Data Protection Regulations
1. **GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)**
   - **Scope:** EU and EEA data subjects
   - **Requirements:** Data protection, privacy rights, breach notification
   - **Compliance:** Data protection by design, privacy impact assessments
   - **Penalties:** Up to 4% of annual turnover or â‚¬20 million
   - **Updates:** Regular guidance from EDPB, national authorities
   - **Monitoring:** Quarterly compliance reviews, annual audits
   - **Training:** Mandatory GDPR training for all staff
   - **Documentation:** Comprehensive data protection documentation

2. **CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)**
   - **Scope:** California residents
   - **Requirements:** Consumer rights, data transparency, opt-out mechanisms
   - **Compliance:** Privacy policy updates, consumer request handling
   - **Penalties:** Up to $7,500 per violation
   - **Updates:** CPRA amendments, regulatory guidance
   - **Monitoring:** Monthly compliance reviews, annual audits
   - **Training:** CCPA-specific training for relevant staff
   - **Documentation:** Privacy notices, data processing records

3. **PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act)**
   - **Scope:** Canadian personal information
   - **Requirements:** Consent, purpose limitation, data minimization
   - **Compliance:** Privacy policies, consent mechanisms
   - **Penalties:** Up to $100,000 per violation
   - **Updates:** Regular guidance from Privacy Commissioner
   - **Monitoring:** Quarterly compliance reviews, annual audits
   - **Training:** PIPEDA training for Canadian operations
   - **Documentation:** Privacy policies, consent records

#### Industry-Specific Regulations
1. **HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)**
   - **Scope:** Healthcare information
   - **Requirements:** Administrative, physical, technical safeguards
   - **Compliance:** Risk assessments, security measures, training
   - **Penalties:** Up to $1.5 million per violation
   - **Updates:** HHS guidance, enforcement actions
   - **Monitoring:** Monthly compliance reviews, annual audits
   - **Training:** HIPAA training for healthcare staff
   - **Documentation:** Policies, procedures, risk assessments

2. **PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard)**
   - **Scope:** Payment card data
   - **Requirements:** 12 security requirements, regular assessments
   - **Compliance:** Security controls, vulnerability management
   - **Penalties:** Fines, loss of processing privileges
   - **Updates:** PCI SSC guidance, new requirements
   - **Monitoring:** Quarterly assessments, annual audits
   - **Training:** PCI DSS training for payment staff
   - **Documentation:** Security policies, assessment reports

3. **SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley Act)**
   - **Scope:** Public companies
   - **Requirements:** Internal controls, financial reporting
   - **Compliance:** Control frameworks, testing, documentation
   - **Penalties:** Criminal and civil penalties
   - **Updates:** SEC guidance, PCAOB standards
   - **Monitoring:** Quarterly reviews, annual audits
   - **Training:** SOX training for finance and IT staff
   - **Documentation:** Control documentation, test results
