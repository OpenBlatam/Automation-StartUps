## A/B Testing Strategy

### Test 1: Email 1 Subject Lines
**Test Duration:** 7 days
**Sample Size:** 1,000 subscribers per variant
**Success Metric:** Open rate + reply rate
**Winner Criteria:** Highest combined engagement

### Test 2: Email 2 Subject Lines
**Test Duration:** 7 days
**Sample Size:** 1,000 subscribers per variant
**Success Metric:** Open rate + click-through rate
**Winner Criteria:** Highest CTR with >25% open rate

### Test 3: Email 3 Subject Lines
**Test Duration:** 7 days
**Sample Size:** 1,000 subscribers per variant
**Success Metric:** Open rate + stay rate
**Winner Criteria:** Highest stay rate with >30% open rate

## Testing Recommendations

1. **Start with Original Versions:** Test the original subject lines first
2. **Test One at a Time:** Don't test multiple emails simultaneously
3. **Track Beyond Opens:** Monitor click-through rates and conversions
4. **Segment Results:** Analyze performance by subscriber type
5. **Document Winners:** Keep track of winning variants for future campaigns
