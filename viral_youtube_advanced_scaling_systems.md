### **Scaling Strategy Framework**
Scaling a viral YouTube business from solo creator to enterprise-level operation requires systematic approaches to team building, process optimization, and infrastructure development. This framework provides comprehensive scaling strategies for sustainable growth.

### **Scaling Phases**
1. **Solo Creator (0-10K subscribers)**: Individual content creation and basic systems
2. **Small Team (10K-100K subscribers)**: 2-5 person team with specialized roles
3. **Medium Team (100K-1M subscribers)**: 5-15 person team with departments
4. **Large Team (1M+ subscribers)**: 15+ person team with full organizational structure
5. **Enterprise Level (5M+ subscribers)**: 50+ person organization with multiple divisions
