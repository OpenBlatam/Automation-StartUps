## Vendor Risk Assessment Framework

### Risk Categories

#### Critical Vendors
- **Definition:** Vendors whose failure would cause significant business impact
- **Examples:** Cloud providers, payment processors, AI services
- **Risk Level:** High
- **Monitoring:** Continuous
- **Backup:** Required

**Critical Vendor List:**
- **AWS:** Cloud infrastructure
- **OpenAI:** AI services
- **Stripe:** Payment processing
- **Zoom:** Video conferencing
- **SendGrid:** Email services

#### Important Vendors
- **Definition:** Vendors whose failure would cause moderate business impact
- **Examples:** CDN providers, monitoring services, communication tools
- **Risk Level:** Medium
- **Monitoring:** Regular
- **Backup:** Recommended

**Important Vendor List:**
- **Cloudflare:** CDN and security
- **DataDog:** Monitoring services
- **Slack:** Communication platform
- **StatusPage.io:** Status page services
- **PagerDuty:** Alerting services

#### Standard Vendors
- **Definition:** Vendors whose failure would cause minimal business impact
- **Examples:** Office supplies, marketing tools, administrative services
- **Risk Level:** Low
- **Monitoring:** Periodic
- **Backup:** Optional

**Standard Vendor List:**
- **Office Supplies:** Stationery and equipment
- **Marketing Tools:** Design and content tools
- **Administrative Services:** Legal and accounting
- **Training Services:** Professional development
- **Consulting Services:** Business consulting

### Risk Scoring

#### Risk Score Calculation
**Formula:** Risk Score = (Impact × Probability × Vulnerability) / 3

**Impact Levels:**
- **High (3):** >$100,000 impact
- **Medium (2):** $10,000 - $100,000 impact
- **Low (1):** <$10,000 impact

**Probability Levels:**
- **High (3):** >50% probability
- **Medium (2):** 10-50% probability
- **Low (1):** <10% probability

**Vulnerability Levels:**
- **High (3):** Multiple vulnerabilities
- **Medium (2):** Some vulnerabilities
- **Low (1):** Few vulnerabilities

#### Risk Rating
- **Critical (9):** Immediate action required
- **High (6-8):** Action required within 30 days
- **Medium (3-5):** Action required within 90 days
- **Low (1-2):** Monitor and review
