## Executive Summary

## Disaster Recovery Framework

### Disaster Classification

#### Level 1: Minor Disaster
- **Definition:** Limited system failure with minimal business impact
- **Examples:** Single server failure, minor data corruption
- **Recovery Time Objective (RTO):** 4 hours
- **Recovery Point Objective (RPO):** 1 hour
- **Response Team:** Technical Team

#### Level 2: Moderate Disaster
- **Definition:** Multiple system failures with moderate business impact
- **Examples:** Data center outage, major system failure
- **Recovery Time Objective (RTO):** 24 hours
- **Recovery Point Objective (RPO):** 4 hours
- **Response Team:** Technical Team + Management

#### Level 3: Major Disaster
- **Definition:** Complete system failure with major business impact
- **Examples:** Complete data center loss, natural disaster
- **Recovery Time Objective (RTO):** 72 hours
- **Recovery Point Objective (RPO):** 24 hours
- **Response Team:** Full Technical Team + Executive Team

#### Level 4: Catastrophic Disaster
- **Definition:** Complete business disruption with catastrophic impact
- **Examples:** Regional disaster, cyber attack, pandemic
- **Recovery Time Objective (RTO):** 7 days
- **Recovery Point Objective (RPO):** 72 hours
- **Response Team:** Full Organization + External Support
