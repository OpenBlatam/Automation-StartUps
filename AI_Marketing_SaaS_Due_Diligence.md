## E) CUSTOMER INTERVIEWS (3 Critical Questions)

### Power User Interview Framework

1. **"What specific marketing challenge does our AI platform solve that you couldn't address with HubSpot or other tools?"**
   - *Purpose*: Validate unique value proposition
   - *Success Metric*: Clear differentiation from competitors
   - *Red Flag*: "It's just like HubSpot but cheaper"

2. **"If our platform disappeared tomorrow, what would be your backup plan, and how much would it cost you in time/money?"**
   - *Purpose*: Assess switching costs and dependency
   - *Success Metric*: High switching costs, significant impact
   - *Red Flag*: Easy substitution with minimal impact

3. **"What's the ROI you've seen from our AI features, and what would make you upgrade to our enterprise tier?"**
   - *Purpose*: Validate value delivery and expansion potential
   - *Success Metric*: Quantifiable ROI, clear upgrade path
   - *Red Flag*: Vague benefits, no expansion interest
