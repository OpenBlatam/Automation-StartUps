### 2. Rapid Response System (RRS)
**Immediate Response Protocol (0-2 hours):**
```
Phase 1: Crisis Detection & Assessment (0-30 minutes)
□ Activate crisis response team
□ Assess situation severity and scope
□ Identify key stakeholders and audiences
□ Gather initial facts and information
□ Determine initial response strategy

Phase 2: Initial Response (30-60 minutes)
□ Issue holding statement
□ Notify key stakeholders
□ Activate media monitoring
□ Prepare initial media response
□ Set up crisis communication center

Phase 3: Strategic Response (1-2 hours)
□ Develop comprehensive response strategy
□ Prepare detailed statements
□ Coordinate with legal and PR teams
□ Engage with media and stakeholders
□ Monitor and adjust response
```

**Advanced Response Templates:**
```
Template 1: Immediate Holding Statement
"We are aware of [situation] and are taking immediate action to [response]. 
Our priority is [key message]. We will provide updates as we have more information. 
For immediate questions, contact [spokesperson] at [contact information]."

Template 2: Detailed Response Statement
"We have been made aware of [situation] and are conducting a thorough investigation. 
Our immediate priority is [primary concern]. We are working with [authorities/experts] 
to [action taken]. We will provide regular updates as we learn more. 
We are committed to [values/principles] and will take appropriate action."

Template 3: Recovery and Moving Forward
"We have completed our investigation into [situation] and have implemented 
[corrective actions]. We have learned valuable lessons and are committed to 
[improvements]. We will continue to [ongoing actions] and will provide 
updates on our progress."
```
