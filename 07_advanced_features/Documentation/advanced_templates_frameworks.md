## ROI Calculation Frameworks

### 1. Content Marketing ROI Calculator
```excel
# Content Marketing ROI Framework

## Input Variables
- Content Creation Cost: $[Amount]
- Content Distribution Cost: $[Amount]
- Time Investment (Hours): [Number]
- Hourly Rate: $[Amount]

## Output Metrics
- Organic Traffic Increase: [Percentage]
- Lead Generation: [Number of leads]
- Conversion Rate: [Percentage]
- Average Customer Value: $[Amount]

## ROI Calculation
Total Investment = Content Creation + Distribution + (Time × Hourly Rate)
Revenue Generated = Leads × Conversion Rate × Average Customer Value
ROI = (Revenue Generated - Total Investment) / Total Investment × 100

## Break-even Analysis
Break-even Leads = Total Investment / (Conversion Rate × Average Customer Value)
Break-even Time = [Months to achieve break-even]

- [ ] Channel effectiveness review
- [ ] Audience targeting refinement
- [ ] Content format optimization
```
