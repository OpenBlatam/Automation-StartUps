# Industry-Specific Investor Deck Templates

## üè≠ Sector-Specific Frameworks

### SaaS/Technology Companies
**Unique Considerations**: Recurring revenue, unit economics, scalability, churn rates

**Enhanced Framework**:
```
1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
   - ARR growth rate and trajectory
   - Key SaaS metrics (MRR, churn, LTV/CAC)
   - Product-market fit indicators
   - Scalability and expansion potential

2. PRODUCT & TECHNOLOGY
   - Platform architecture and scalability
   - API ecosystem and integrations
   - Security and compliance features
   - Product roadmap and innovation pipeline

3. BUSINESS MODEL
   - Subscription tiers and pricing strategy
   - Revenue recognition and billing
   - Customer acquisition channels
   - Expansion revenue and upsell opportunities

4. TRACTION & METRICS
   - Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) growth
   - Customer acquisition cost (CAC) trends
   - Lifetime value (LTV) and payback period
   - Net Revenue Retention (NRR) and expansion

5. MARKET OPPORTUNITY
   - Total Addressable Market (TAM) for software
   - Digital transformation trends
   - Competitive landscape and differentiation
   - Market timing and adoption curves
```

**ClickUp Brain Prompts for SaaS**:
```
"Extract SaaS-specific insights from linked documents:

SAAS METRICS ANALYSIS:
1. REVENUE METRICS: MRR, ARR, growth rates, and trends
2. CUSTOMER METRICS: CAC, LTV, churn rates, and retention
3. PRODUCT METRICS: Usage, engagement, and feature adoption
4. OPERATIONAL METRICS: Support costs, infrastructure, and efficiency
5. MARKET METRICS: Competitive positioning and market share

Format as investor-ready SaaS metrics with benchmarks and context."
```
