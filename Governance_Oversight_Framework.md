## Executive Summary

This document outlines the governance and oversight framework for our business continuity plan, establishing clear roles, responsibilities, and accountability structures for effective plan management and execution.

## Governance Structure

### Board of Directors

#### Board Responsibilities
- **Strategic Oversight:** Overall business continuity strategy
- **Resource Allocation:** Budget and resource approval
- **Risk Oversight:** Enterprise risk management
- **Performance Monitoring:** Plan effectiveness evaluation
- **Stakeholder Management:** External stakeholder relations

#### Board Committees
1. **Risk Management Committee**
   - **Chair:** Independent Director
   - **Members:** 3-5 board members
   - **Responsibilities:** Risk oversight, risk strategy, risk reporting
   - **Meeting Frequency:** Quarterly
   - **Reporting:** Board of Directors

2. **Audit Committee**
   - **Chair:** Independent Director
   - **Members:** 3-5 board members
   - **Responsibilities:** Financial oversight, compliance, internal audit
   - **Meeting Frequency:** Quarterly
   - **Reporting:** Board of Directors

3. **Technology Committee**
   - **Chair:** Technology Director
   - **Members:** 3-5 board members
   - **Responsibilities:** Technology strategy, cybersecurity, innovation
   - **Meeting Frequency:** Quarterly
   - **Reporting:** Board of Directors

### Executive Leadership

#### CEO Responsibilities
- **Overall Leadership:** Business continuity leadership
- **Strategic Direction:** Plan strategy and direction

## Decision-Making Framework

### Decision-Making Hierarchy

#### Strategic Decisions
- **Authority:** Board of Directors
- **Scope:** Strategic direction, major investments, risk tolerance
- **Timeline:** Quarterly or as needed
- **Documentation:** Board resolutions, strategic plans
- **Approval:** Board approval required
- **Communication:** All stakeholders
- **Review:** Annual strategic review
- **Continuous Improvement:** Strategic planning process

#### Operational Decisions
- **Authority:** Executive Team
- **Scope:** Operational procedures, resource allocation, performance management
- **Timeline:** Monthly or as needed
- **Documentation:** Executive decisions, operational plans
- **Approval:** CEO approval required
- **Communication:** Management team
- **Review:** Quarterly operational review
- **Continuous Improvement:** Operational planning process

#### Tactical Decisions
- **Authority:** Management Team
- **Scope:** Day-to-day operations, process improvements, team management
- **Timeline:** Weekly or as needed
- **Documentation:** Management decisions, process documentation
- **Approval:** Department head approval required
- **Communication:** Team members
- **Review:** Monthly tactical review
- **Continuous Improvement:** Process improvement process
