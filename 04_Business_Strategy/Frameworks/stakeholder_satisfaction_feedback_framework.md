## Satisfaction Framework

### Satisfaction Measurement

#### Satisfaction Metrics
1. **Overall Satisfaction**
   - **Target:** >4.5/5 rating
   - **Measurement:** Net Promoter Score (NPS), Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT)
   - **Frequency:** Monthly surveys, quarterly reviews
   - **Reporting:** Executive dashboards, management reports
   - **Action Threshold:** <4.0/5 rating

2. **Service Satisfaction**
   - **Target:** >4.5/5 rating
   - **Measurement:** Service quality, delivery, support
   - **Frequency:** Monthly surveys, quarterly reviews
   - **Reporting:** Service dashboards, operational reports
   - **Action Threshold:** <4.0/5 rating

3. **Communication Satisfaction**
   - **Target:** >4.5/5 rating
   - **Measurement:** Communication clarity, frequency, effectiveness
   - **Frequency:** Monthly surveys, quarterly reviews
   - **Reporting:** Communication dashboards, stakeholder reports
   - **Action Threshold:** <4.0/5 rating

4. **Training Satisfaction**
   - **Target:** >4.5/5 rating
   - **Measurement:** Training quality, relevance, effectiveness
   - **Frequency:** Post-training surveys, quarterly reviews
   - **Reporting:** Training dashboards, learning reports
   - **Action Threshold:** <4.0/5 rating
