## Revenue Model Framework

### Multi-Stream Revenue Strategy
```
Revenue Streams:
1. Subscription Revenue (40% of total revenue)
   - AI Course Platform: $50-200/month per user
   - Marketing SaaS: $100-1,500/month per user
   - Document Generation: $0.10-0.50 per document

2. Transaction Revenue (25% of total revenue)
   - AI Model Usage: Pay-per-use AI models
   - API Consumption: API usage fees
   - Data Processing: Data processing fees
   - Custom Development: Custom AI solutions

3. Marketplace Revenue (20% of total revenue)
   - AI Model Marketplace: 30% commission on model sales
   - AI Application Store: 30% commission on app sales
   - AI Data Marketplace: 20% commission on data sales
   - AI Service Marketplace: 25% commission on services

4. Enterprise Revenue (15% of total revenue)
   - Enterprise Licenses: $10,000-100,000/year
   - Custom Solutions: $50,000-500,000 per project
   - Professional Services: $200-500/hour
   - Training and Certification: $1,000-10,000 per person
```

### Pricing Strategy Framework
```
Pricing Models:
- Freemium: Free basic services with premium upgrades
- Usage-Based: Pay-per-use pricing model
- Subscription: Monthly and annual subscriptions
- Enterprise: Enterprise pricing and contracts
- Marketplace: Commission-based marketplace pricing
- Value-Based: Value-based pricing models
```
