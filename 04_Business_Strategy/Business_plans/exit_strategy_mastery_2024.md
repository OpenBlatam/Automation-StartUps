## ðŸŽ¯ **EXIT STRATEGY FRAMEWORK**

### **The 5-Phase Exit Strategy Process**

#### **Phase 1: Exit Planning (0-12 months)**
- **Goal:** Develop comprehensive exit strategy
- **Activities:** Market analysis, valuation assessment, exit readiness evaluation
- **Duration:** 0-12 months
- **Success Metrics:** Exit strategy developed, readiness assessed

#### **Phase 2: Exit Preparation (12-24 months)**
- **Goal:** Prepare company for exit
- **Activities:** Financial optimization, operational improvement, market positioning
- **Duration:** 12-24 months
- **Success Metrics:** Company prepared, value optimized

#### **Phase 3: Exit Marketing (24-36 months)**
- **Goal:** Market company to potential acquirers
- **Activities:** Buyer identification, marketing materials, due diligence preparation
- **Duration:** 24-36 months
- **Success Metrics:** Buyers identified, marketing materials prepared

#### **Phase 4: Exit Negotiation (36-42 months)**
- **Goal:** Negotiate and execute exit
- **Activities:** Term sheet negotiation, due diligence, closing
- **Duration:** 36-42 months
- **Success Metrics:** Exit executed, value maximized

#### **Phase 5: Exit Transition (42+ months)**
- **Goal:** Ensure smooth transition
- **Activities:** Integration planning, stakeholder management, value realization
- **Duration:** 42+ months
- **Success Metrics:** Smooth transition, value realized
