#### Scaling Phases

**Team Size**: 5-10 employees
**Focus**: Product development and initial market entry

**Key Activities**:
- Core team building and hiring
- Product development and MVP
- Initial marketing and customer acquisition
- Culture development and values
- Process establishment and optimization

##### Phase 2: Growth (Months 13-24)
**Team Size**: 15-25 employees
**Focus**: Market expansion and customer success

**Key Activities**:
- Team scaling and expansion
- Market penetration and growth
- Customer success and retention
- Process optimization and automation
- Culture scaling and development

##### Phase 3: Scale (Months 25-36)
**Team Size**: 30-50 employees
**Focus**: Market leadership and international expansion

**Key Activities**:
- Team scaling and international expansion
- Market leadership and competitive advantage
- Innovation and product development
- Culture evolution and development
- Process optimization and automation

#### Scaling Challenges & Solutions

##### Challenge 1: Maintaining Culture
**Solution**: Strong values, regular communication, and culture initiatives

##### Challenge 2: Communication and Coordination
**Solution**: Clear processes, regular meetings, and collaboration tools

##### Challenge 3: Quality and Performance
**Solution**: Strong hiring, training, and performance management

##### Challenge 4: Innovation and Creativity
**Solution**: Innovation time, experimentation, and recognition

##### Challenge 5: Retention and Engagement
**Solution**: Competitive compensation, growth opportunities, and recognition
