# VC Screening Quick Reference Card
## 1-Page Evaluation Guide

### Scoring Scale (1-10)
**10**: Exceptional | **8-9**: Strong | **6-7**: Good | **4-5**: Fair | **1-3**: Poor

### Core Evaluation Criteria

#### 1. PROBLEM (25% weight)
- **Pain Intensity**: How acute is the problem?
- **Market Size**: TAM/SAM/SOM analysis
- **Market Timing**: Is the market ready?

#### 2. SOLUTION (20% weight)
- **Technical Moat**: Proprietary technology/IP
- **Network Effects**: Value increases with users
- **Data Moat**: Proprietary data advantage
- **Switching Costs**: Customer lock-in

#### 3. TRACTION (20% weight)
- **MRR Growth**: Month-over-month growth
- **User Growth**: Customer acquisition rate
- **Revenue Quality**: Recurring vs. one-time
- **Market Validation**: Customer feedback

#### 4. TEAM (15% weight)
- **Domain Expertise**: Relevant experience
- **Execution Track Record**: Prior successes
- **Team Completeness**: Key roles filled
- **Leadership Quality**: Vision and communication

#### 5. UNIT ECONOMICS (10% weight)
- **CAC**: Customer acquisition cost
- **LTV**: Lifetime value
- **LTV/CAC Ratio**: Unit economics efficiency
- **Burn Rate**: Monthly cash consumption

#### 6. ASK (5% weight)
- **Valuation**: Fair market value
- **Use of Funds**: Clear allocation plan
- **Runway**: Time to next milestone

#### 7. RED FLAGS (5% weight)
- **Legal/Regulatory**: Compliance issues
- **Competition**: Market saturation
- **Technology Risk**: Technical feasibility
- **Market Risk**: External factors

### Decision Framework
- **9-10**: PASS (Exceptional opportunity)
- **7-8**: DEEP DIVE (Strong opportunity)
- **5-6**: DEEP DIVE (Caution) (Moderate opportunity)
- **3-4**: REJECT (Weak opportunity)
- **1-2**: REJECT (Poor opportunity)

### Sector-Specific Focus Areas

#### AI Startups
- Data moats and proprietary datasets
- Technical talent and research capabilities
- AI model performance and accuracy
- Scalability of AI infrastructure

#### Climate Startups
- Technology readiness level (TRL)
- Carbon impact measurement
- Regulatory tailwinds
- Government incentives

#### Fintech Startups
- Regulatory compliance
- Security and risk management
- Banking partnerships
- Customer trust and adoption

### Key Red Flags
- No clear competitive advantage
- Poor unit economics
- Limited market opportunity
- Weak team or execution risk
- Major regulatory issues
- High customer concentration
- Unsustainable burn rate

### Due Diligence Checklist
- [ ] Customer reference calls
- [ ] Technical review
- [ ] Financial audit
- [ ] Legal review
- [ ] Market validation
- [ ] Competitive analysis
- [ ] IP review
- [ ] Team references
