- **Model**: Direct sales with dedicated account managers
- **Team**: 5 enterprise sales reps
- **Process**: 6-9 month sales cycle
- **Tools**: CRM, sales enablement, demos
- **Investment**: $1.5M annually

#### **Mid-Market Sales**
- **Model**: Inside sales with product-led growth
- **Team**: 8 inside sales reps
- **Process**: 3-6 month sales cycle
- **Tools**: CRM, automation, trials
- **Investment**: $800K annually

#### **SMB Sales**
- **Model**: Self-service with minimal sales support
- **Team**: 2 sales support reps
- **Process**: 1-3 month sales cycle
- **Tools**: Self-service, chat support
- **Investment**: $200K annually

### **Sales Process**

#### **Enterprise Process**
1. **Prospecting**: Account-based marketing
2. **Qualification**: BANT criteria
3. **Discovery**: Needs assessment
4. **Demo**: Custom demonstration
5. **Proposal**: Value-based pricing
6. **Negotiation**: Contract terms
7. **Close**: Implementation planning

#### **Mid-Market Process**
1. **Lead Generation**: Content marketing
2. **Qualification**: Lead scoring
3. **Demo**: Standard demonstration
4. **Trial**: Free trial period
5. **Follow-up**: Sales enablement
6. **Close**: Standard pricing

#### **SMB Process**
1. **Discovery**: Website, content
2. **Trial**: Self-service signup
3. **Onboarding**: Automated process
4. **Support**: Chat, email support
5. **Upgrade**: Feature-based upsells
