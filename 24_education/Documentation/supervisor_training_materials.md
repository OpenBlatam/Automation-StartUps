### üéØ **Feedback Framework**

#### **The SBI Model (Situation-Behavior-Impact)**
```
Situation: "During yesterday's webinar session..."
Behavior: "You used interactive polls every 10 minutes..."
Impact: "This resulted in 85% participant engagement, which exceeded our target."
```

#### **Feedback Types**

##### **Positive Feedback**
- **Specific**: "Your use of the breakout room activity increased participation by 40%"
- **Timely**: Provide within 24 hours of the behavior
- **Meaningful**: Connect to larger goals and impact
- **Actionable**: Suggest how to build on the success

##### **Constructive Feedback**
- **Balanced**: Start with positive, address improvement area, end with positive
- **Specific**: Focus on behavior, not personality
- **Solution-focused**: Provide clear next steps
- **Supportive**: Offer resources and assistance

#### **Feedback Delivery Techniques**

##### **The Feedback Sandwich**
1. **Positive**: Start with something done well
2. **Improvement**: Address the area needing work
3. **Positive**: End with encouragement and support

##### **The GROW Model**
- **Goal**: What does the employee want to achieve?
- **Reality**: What is the current situation?
- **Options**: What are the possible solutions?
- **Will**: What will the employee commit to doing?

### üìä **Performance Improvement Process**

#### **Step 1: Identify the Issue**
- Review metrics and performance data
- Gather feedback from multiple sources
- Identify root causes, not just symptoms
- Consider external factors and circumstances

#### **Step 2: Develop Improvement Plan**
- Set specific, measurable goals
- Identify required resources and support
- Create timeline with milestones
- Define success criteria

#### **Step 3: Implement and Monitor**
- Provide necessary training and resources
- Monitor progress regularly
- Adjust plan as needed
- Celebrate improvements and milestones

#### **Step 4: Evaluate and Adjust**
- Assess progress against goals
- Gather feedback on the improvement process
- Identify lessons learned
- Plan next steps and continued development

### üö® **Common Issues & Solutions**

#### **Technical Issues**

##### **System Access Problems**
| Issue | Symptoms | Quick Fix | Supervisor Action |
|-------|----------|-----------|------------------|
| **Login Failures** | Can't access platform | Clear cache, reset password | Contact IT support, provide temporary access |
| **Slow Performance** | System runs slowly | Check internet, close other apps | Monitor usage, provide alternative tools |
| **Feature Not Working** | Specific function fails | Refresh page, try different browser | Report to IT, find workaround |

##### **Gamification Issues**
| Issue | Symptoms | Quick Fix | Supervisor Action |
|-------|----------|-----------|------------------|
| **Points Not Updating** | Achievements not recorded | Refresh dashboard, check sync | Verify completion, manual update if needed |
| **Badges Not Appearing** | Earned badge not showing | Wait 24 hours, contact support | Confirm achievement, escalate if needed |
| **Progress Not Tracking** | Tasks not marked complete | Check task status, resubmit | Review completion, provide guidance |

#### **Employee Issues**

##### **Engagement Problems**
| Issue | Signs | Supervisor Response | Prevention |
|-------|-------|-------------------|------------|
| **Low Participation** | Minimal task completion | Schedule 1-on-1, identify barriers | Regular check-ins, clear expectations |
| **Frustration** | Negative comments, resistance | Listen actively, address concerns | Provide support, manage expectations |
| **Confusion** | Many questions, repeated mistakes | Provide clarification, additional training | Clear instructions, regular feedback |

##### **Performance Issues**
| Issue | Signs | Supervisor Response | Prevention |
|-------|-------|-------------------|------------|
| **Slow Progress** | Behind on tasks, missed deadlines | Identify obstacles, provide support | Regular monitoring, early intervention |
| **Quality Concerns** | Poor work quality, errors | Review standards, provide training | Clear expectations, quality examples |
| **Motivation Loss** | Decreased effort, negative attitude | Discuss goals, find motivators | Regular recognition, meaningful work |

### üö® **Escalation Procedures**

#### **Level 1: Supervisor Resolution**
- **Scope**: Common issues, minor problems
- **Action**: Use standard procedures and resources
- **Timeline**: Resolve within 24 hours
- **Documentation**: Record issue and resolution

#### **Level 2: HR Support**
- **Scope**: Complex issues, policy questions
- **Action**: Contact HR team for guidance
- **Timeline**: Response within 4 hours
- **Documentation**: Formal incident report

#### **Level 3: Management Escalation**
- **Scope**: Serious issues, system failures
- **Action**: Escalate to department head
- **Timeline**: Response within 2 hours
- **Documentation**: Executive summary and action plan

#### **Level 4: Emergency Response**
- **Scope**: Critical issues, security concerns
- **Action**: Contact emergency hotline
- **Timeline**: Immediate response
- **Documentation**: Incident report and follow-up

### üåü **Best Practices for Success**

#### **Relationship Building**
- **First Day Focus**: Make new employees feel welcome and valued
- **Regular Check-ins**: Schedule consistent, meaningful conversations
- **Active Listening**: Give full attention and show genuine interest
- **Personal Connection**: Learn about interests, goals, and motivations

#### **Communication Excellence**
- **Clarity**: Use clear, simple language and avoid jargon
- **Consistency**: Maintain consistent messaging and expectations
- **Transparency**: Share relevant information and context
- **Accessibility**: Be available and approachable

#### **Development Focus**
- **Growth Mindset**: Encourage learning and improvement
- **Skill Building**: Identify and develop key competencies
- **Career Planning**: Discuss long-term goals and opportunities
- **Mentorship**: Provide guidance and support for development

### üéØ **Advanced Coaching Techniques**

#### **The Coaching Conversation Model**
1. **Opening**: Set the tone and agenda
2. **Discovery**: Ask open-ended questions
3. **Insight**: Help employee gain new understanding
4. **Action**: Develop specific next steps
5. **Commitment**: Ensure accountability and follow-up

#### **Powerful Questions**
- **Discovery Questions**: "What's working well for you?"
- **Insight Questions**: "What patterns do you notice?"
- **Action Questions**: "What would you like to try differently?"
- **Commitment Questions**: "What will you commit to doing?"

#### **Active Listening Skills**
- **Full Attention**: Focus completely on the employee
- **Reflective Listening**: Paraphrase what you heard
- **Clarifying Questions**: Ask for more information
- **Empathetic Response**: Acknowledge feelings and concerns

### üèÜ **Success Strategies**

- **Psychological Safety**: Encourage risk-taking and learning
- **Recognition Culture**: Celebrate achievements and efforts
- **Supportive Atmosphere**: Provide help and encouragement

#### **Building Confidence**
- **Small Wins**: Celebrate incremental progress
- **Skill Development**: Provide training and practice opportunities
- **Positive Feedback**: Focus on strengths and improvements

#### **Long-term Success**
