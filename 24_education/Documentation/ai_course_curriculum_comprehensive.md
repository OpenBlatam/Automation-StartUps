# AI Course: Comprehensive Curriculum for Modern Professionals

## Course Overview

**Title**: "AI Mastery: From Zero to Expert in the Age of Artificial Intelligence"
**Duration**: 12 weeks (3 months)
**Format**: Hybrid (Online + Live Sessions)
**Target Audience**: Business professionals, entrepreneurs, marketers, and decision-makers
**Prerequisites**: Basic computer literacy, no prior AI experience required

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

1. **Understand AI Fundamentals**: Grasp core AI concepts, terminology, and current capabilities
2. **Apply AI in Business**: Implement AI solutions for marketing, operations, and decision-making
3. **Navigate AI Tools**: Master popular AI platforms and tools for professional use
4. **Develop AI Strategy**: Create comprehensive AI adoption strategies for organizations
5. **Ethical AI Practice**: Understand and implement ethical AI principles
6. **Future-Proof Skills**: Stay ahead of AI trends and emerging technologies

### **Project Requirements:**
Students will complete a comprehensive AI project that demonstrates their mastery of course concepts and practical application skills.

4. **AI Research Project**: Conduct original research on an AI topic of interest

### **Project Deliverables:**
