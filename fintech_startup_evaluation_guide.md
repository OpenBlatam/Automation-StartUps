# Fintech Startup Evaluation Guide
## Sector-Specific Criteria for Fintech Investments

### Key Fintech Evaluation Dimensions

#### 1. Regulatory Compliance (Critical)
**Licensing & Permits**
- Required financial licenses
- Regulatory approval status
- Compliance with banking regulations
- International regulatory considerations

**Risk Management**
- Fraud prevention capabilities
- Security and data protection
- Anti-money laundering (AML) compliance
- Know Your Customer (KYC) processes

#### 2. Market Opportunity (Critical)
**Market Size & Growth**
- Total addressable market for financial services
- Market growth rate and drivers
- Customer adoption patterns
- Market timing and readiness

**Customer Segments**
- B2B vs. B2C focus
- Enterprise vs. SMB targeting
- Customer concentration risk
- Sales cycle characteristics

#### 3. Technology & Innovation (Important)
**Technical Differentiation**
- Novel approaches to financial problems
- API and integration capabilities
- Scalability and performance
- Security and compliance features

**Product-Market Fit**
- Clear value proposition
- Customer retention metrics
- Usage patterns and engagement
- Feature adoption rates

#### 4. Business Model (Important)
**Revenue Model**
- Transaction fees vs. subscription
- Recurring vs. one-time revenue
- Pricing strategy and elasticity
- Customer lifetime value

**Unit Economics**
- Cost structure and margins
- Customer acquisition costs
- Transaction processing costs
- Path to profitability

### Fintech-Specific Red Flags

#### Regulatory Red Flags
- Unlicensed operations
- Regulatory violations
- Compliance gaps
- International regulatory issues
- Security vulnerabilities

#### Market Red Flags
- High customer acquisition costs
- Poor unit economics
- Limited market size
- Competitive pressure from banks
- Long sales cycles

#### Business Red Flags
- Poor customer retention
- High churn rates
- Limited scalability
- Customer concentration risk
- Dependency on single partners

### Fintech Sector Benchmarks

#### Early Stage (Pre-Seed/Seed)
- **Team**: 2-5 people, strong technical founders
- **Traction**: 0-50 customers, early pilots
- **Revenue**: $0-100K MRR
- **Valuation**: $5-25M pre-money

#### Growth Stage (Series A)
- **Team**: 10-25 people, complete founding team
- **Traction**: 50-500 customers, product-market fit
- **Revenue**: $100K-2M MRR
- **Valuation**: $25-150M pre-money

#### Scale Stage (Series B)
- **Team**: 25-100 people, experienced management
- **Traction**: 500+ customers, strong growth
- **Revenue**: $2M+ MRR
- **Valuation**: $150M+ pre-money

### Key Questions for Fintech Startups

#### Regulatory Questions
1. What licenses do you need to operate?
2. How do you ensure regulatory compliance?
3. What's your approach to risk management?
4. How do you handle security and fraud?
5. What are your international expansion plans?

#### Business Questions
1. What's the ROI for your customers?
2. How do you price your financial services?
3. What are your customer acquisition costs?
4. How do you handle enterprise sales cycles?
5. What's your competitive differentiation?

#### Market Questions
1. How large is your addressable market?
2. What's the adoption timeline for your solution?
3. How do you handle regulatory compliance?
4. What partnerships are critical to your success?
5. How do you scale your go-to-market strategy?
