## Audit Framework

### Audit Types

#### Internal Audits
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Scope:** Plan effectiveness, compliance, performance
- **Auditor:** Internal audit team
- **Duration:** 2-4 weeks
- **Reporting:** Management team

#### External Audits
- **Frequency:** Annually
- **Scope:** Regulatory compliance, industry standards
- **Auditor:** External audit firm
- **Duration:** 4-6 weeks
- **Reporting:** Board of Directors

#### Regulatory Audits
- **Frequency:** As required by regulators
- **Scope:** Regulatory compliance, data protection
- **Auditor:** Regulatory authorities
- **Duration:** 2-8 weeks
- **Reporting:** Regulatory authorities

#### Certification Audits
- **Frequency:** Every 3 years
- **Scope:** ISO 22301, SOC 2, other certifications
- **Auditor:** Certification body
- **Duration:** 2-4 weeks
- **Reporting:** Certification body
