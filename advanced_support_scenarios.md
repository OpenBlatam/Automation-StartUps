- Acknowledge the customer's emotional state

- Offer multiple solution paths
- Provide immediate actionable steps
- Set clear expectations for resolution

- Focus on relationship over transaction
- Offer additional value to compensate for issues
- Turn problems into opportunities for deeper engagement

- Provide specific timelines and follow-up
