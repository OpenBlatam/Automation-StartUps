## D) LEGAL DUE DILIGENCE

### Cap Table Review
- [ ] **Founder Equity**: Percentage and vesting schedules
- [ ] **Investor Ownership**: Series A/B terms and liquidation preferences
- [ ] **Employee Stock Options**: Pool size and outstanding grants
- [ ] **Advisor Equity**: Vesting and acceleration clauses
- [ ] **Future Dilution**: Impact of planned funding rounds

### Intellectual Property (IP)
- [ ] **Trademarks**: "AI Course Platform" and related marks
- [ ] **Copyrights**: Course content and platform code
- [ ] **Patents**: AI algorithms for document generation
- [ ] **Trade Secrets**: Proprietary AI training methods
- [ ] **Open Source**: Compliance with licenses used

### 409A Valuation
- [ ] **Current Valuation**: Fair market value of common stock
- [ ] **Valuation Date**: Must be within 12 months
- [ ] **Methodology**: Market approach, income approach, asset approach
- [ ] **Key Assumptions**: Revenue projections, market comparables
- [ ] **Next Valuation**: Scheduled date and triggers

## E) CUSTOMER INTERVIEWS (3 Key Questions)

### Power User Interview Questions

1. **"What specific business problem does our AI marketing course solve for you that other platforms don't?"**
   - *Purpose*: Validate unique value proposition
   - *Red Flag*: Generic answers or mentions competitors solving same problem

2. **"How has using our bulk document AI tool changed your workflow, and what would happen if we doubled our pricing?"**
   - *Purpose*: Assess product stickiness and price sensitivity
   - *Red Flag*: Easy substitution or price resistance

3. **"If you were to recommend our platform to a colleague, what would you emphasize, and what would you warn them about?"**
   - *Purpose*: Understand advocacy potential and pain points
   - *Red Flag*: More warnings than benefits
