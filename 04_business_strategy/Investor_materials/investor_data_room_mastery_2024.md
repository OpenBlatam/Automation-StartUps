### **The Psychology of Data Rooms**
```
PSYCHOLOGY PRINCIPLES:
- **Authority**: Professional, complete, organized
- **Transparency**: Open, honest, comprehensive
- **Efficiency**: Easy to navigate, quick access
- **Credibility**: High-quality, accurate, up-to-date
- **Trust**: Secure, confidential, professional

SUCCESS FACTORS:
- Complete documentation
- Professional presentation
- Easy navigation
- Quick access to key information
- Regular updates
- Secure access controls
```

### **Data Room Objectives**
```
PRIMARY OBJECTIVES:
- Facilitate due diligence process
- Demonstrate professionalism
- Build investor confidence
- Accelerate decision making
- Reduce information requests
- Show transparency and trust
- Create competitive advantage
- Maximize closing success

SECONDARY OBJECTIVES:
- Showcase company culture

- Highlight market opportunity
- Show competitive advantages
```

## üìÅ **DATA ROOM STRUCTURE**

### **1. EXECUTIVE DOCUMENTS**

#### **1.1 Business Overview**
- [ ] **Executive Summary** (2 pages)
  - Company mission, vision, and values
  - Market opportunity and positioning
  - Key differentiators and competitive advantages
  - Financial highlights and growth projections
  - Investment ask and use of funds

- [ ] **Company Overview** (5 pages)
  - Company history and founding story
  - Leadership team and organizational structure
  - Advisory board and key advisors
  - Company culture and values
  - Key milestones and achievements

- [ ] **Market Analysis** (10 pages)
  - Total Addressable Market (TAM) analysis
  - Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM) breakdown
  - Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) targets
  - Market growth trends and drivers
  - Competitive landscape analysis

#### **1.2 Investment Materials**
- [ ] **Pitch Deck** (15 slides)
  - Problem and solution
  - Market opportunity
  - Business model
  - Traction and metrics
  - Team and advisors
  - Financial projections
  - Use of funds
  - Exit strategy

- [ ] **Financial Model** (Excel)
  - 5-year financial projections
  - Revenue model and assumptions
  - Cost structure and margins
  - Cash flow projections
  - Scenario analysis
  - Sensitivity analysis

- [ ] **Investment Terms** (PDF)
  - Valuation and terms
  - Use of funds breakdown
  - Investor rights and preferences
  - Board composition
  - Liquidation preferences
  - Anti-dilution provisions
