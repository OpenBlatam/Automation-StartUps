## Executive Summary

This document outlines the performance metrics and key performance indicators (KPIs) framework for measuring and monitoring the effectiveness of our business continuity plan.

## Performance Framework

### Performance Categories

#### Technical Performance
- **System Availability:** Uptime, downtime, service availability
- **System Performance:** Response time, throughput, capacity
- **System Reliability:** Error rates, failure rates, stability
- **System Security:** Security incidents, vulnerabilities, compliance
- **System Recovery:** Recovery time, recovery point, restoration

#### Business Performance
- **Revenue Protection:** Revenue loss prevention, revenue recovery
- **Customer Satisfaction:** Customer satisfaction, customer retention
- **Service Delivery:** Service level achievement, service quality
- **Operational Efficiency:** Process efficiency, resource utilization
- **Stakeholder Satisfaction:** Stakeholder satisfaction, stakeholder retention

#### Process Performance
- **Process Efficiency:** Process speed, process cost, process quality
- **Process Effectiveness:** Process outcome, process value, process impact
- **Process Compliance:** Process adherence, process documentation
- **Process Improvement:** Process enhancement, process innovation
- **Process Continuous Improvement:** Process learning, process adaptation

#### Team Performance
- **Team Effectiveness:** Team productivity, team collaboration
- **Team Skills:** Team competency, team development
- **Team Communication:** Team coordination, team information sharing
- **Team Leadership:** Team management, team decision-making
- **Team Continuous Improvement:** Team learning, team adaptation

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Technical KPIs

#### System Availability KPIs
1. **Uptime Percentage**
   - **Target:** 99.9%
   - **Measurement:** (Total Time - Downtime) / Total Time × 100
   - **Frequency:** Daily, weekly, monthly
   - **Reporting:** Real-time dashboard, weekly reports
   - **Action Threshold:** <99.5%

2. **Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF)**
   - **Target:** >720 hours
   - **Measurement:** Total Operating Time / Number of Failures
   - **Frequency:** Monthly
   - **Reporting:** Monthly reports
   - **Action Threshold:** <168 hours

3. **Mean Time to Recovery (MTTR)**
   - **Target:** <4 hours
   - **Measurement:** Total Recovery Time / Number of Incidents
   - **Frequency:** Monthly
   - **Reporting:** Monthly reports
   - **Action Threshold:** >8 hours

#### System Performance KPIs
1. **Response Time**
   - **Target:** <2 seconds
   - **Measurement:** Average response time
   - **Frequency:** Real-time, daily
   - **Reporting:** Real-time dashboard, daily reports
   - **Action Threshold:** >5 seconds

2. **Throughput**
   - **Target:** >1000 requests/minute
   - **Measurement:** Requests processed per minute
   - **Frequency:** Real-time, daily
   - **Reporting:** Real-time dashboard, daily reports
   - **Action Threshold:** <500 requests/minute

3. **Error Rate**
   - **Target:** <0.1%
   - **Measurement:** (Errors / Total Requests) × 100
   - **Frequency:** Real-time, daily
   - **Reporting:** Real-time dashboard, daily reports
   - **Action Threshold:** >1%

#### System Security KPIs
1. **Security Incident Rate**
   - **Target:** 0 incidents
   - **Measurement:** Number of security incidents
   - **Frequency:** Daily, weekly
   - **Reporting:** Daily reports, weekly reports
   - **Action Threshold:** >0 incidents

2. **Vulnerability Resolution Time**
   - **Target:** <24 hours
   - **Measurement:** Time from discovery to resolution
   - **Frequency:** Weekly
   - **Reporting:** Weekly reports
   - **Action Threshold:** >72 hours

3. **Compliance Score**
   - **Target:** 100%
   - **Measurement:** Compliance percentage
   - **Frequency:** Monthly
   - **Reporting:** Monthly reports
   - **Action Threshold:** <95%
