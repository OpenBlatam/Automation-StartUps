# Tracking and Analytics Framework

## Campaign Performance Metrics

### Primary Success Metrics
1. **Open Rate:** 25%+ target
2. **Click-Through Rate:** 10%+ target
3. **Re-engagement Rate:** 5%+ target
4. **Recapture Rate:** 15%+ target
5. **Unsubscribe Rate:** <5% target

### Secondary Performance Metrics
1. **Reply Rate:** Feedback and engagement
2. **Forward Rate:** Viral sharing potential
3. **Bounce Rate:** List quality indicator
4. **Spam Complaint Rate:** Deliverability health
5. **Revenue Recovery:** Financial impact

---

## Email-Specific Tracking

### Email 1: "We Screwed Up"
**Key Metrics:**
- Open Rate: 25-28% expected
- Reply Rate: 8-12% expected
- Click Rate: 5-8% expected
- Unsubscribe Rate: 2-4% expected

**Tracking Points:**
- Email open (timestamp, device, location)
- Link clicks (which links, frequency)
- Reply engagement (response rate, sentiment)
- Unsubscribe action (reason if provided)

**Success Indicators:**
- High reply rate indicates engagement
- Low unsubscribe rate shows message resonance
- Click-through shows interest in feedback

---

### Email 2: "Here's What You Missed"
**Key Metrics:**
- Open Rate: 28-32% expected
- Click Rate: 12-15% expected
- Forward Rate: 3-5% expected
- Unsubscribe Rate: 3-5% expected

**Tracking Points:**
- Content engagement (time spent reading)
- Link clicks (which content pieces)
- Forward actions (viral potential)
- Unsubscribe actions (timing and reason)

**Success Indicators:**
- High click-through shows value demonstration
- Forward rate indicates shareable content
- Low unsubscribe maintains list health

---

### Email 3: "Goodbye?"
**Key Metrics:**
- Open Rate: 32-36% expected
- Stay Rate: 15-20% expected
- Unsubscribe Rate: 8-12% expected
- Click Rate: 18-22% expected

**Tracking Points:**
- Stay decision (immediate action)
- Unsubscribe decision (timing and reason)
- Link clicks (which option chosen)
- Final engagement (last interaction)

**Success Indicators:**
- High stay rate shows effective value proposition
- Unsubscribe rate indicates healthy list cleaning
- Click-through shows clear decision making

## A/B Testing Framework

### Test 1: Subject Line Variants
**Test Structure:**
- 5 variants per email
- 1,000 subscribers per variant
- 7-day test duration
- Statistical significance: 95%

**Success Metrics:**
- Open rate comparison
- Click-through rate analysis
- Engagement quality assessment
- Revenue impact measurement

---

### Test 2: Content Variations
**Test Structure:**
- 3 content variants per email
- 2,000 subscribers per variant
- 14-day test duration
- Statistical significance: 95%

**Success Metrics:**
- Engagement rate comparison
- Conversion rate analysis
- Revenue per subscriber
- Long-term retention impact

---

### Test 3: Send Time Optimization
**Test Structure:**
- 4 time slots per email
- 1,500 subscribers per variant
- 21-day test duration
- Statistical significance: 95%

**Success Metrics:**
- Open rate by time slot
- Click-through rate by time
- Engagement quality by timing
- Revenue impact by timing
