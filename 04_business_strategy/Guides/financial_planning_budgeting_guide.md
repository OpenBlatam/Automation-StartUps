### **Revenue Forecasting Methodology**

#### **Bottom-Up Forecasting**
- **Customer Acquisition**: Project new customer acquisition
- **Customer Retention**: Model customer retention and churn
- **Usage Growth**: Forecast usage and consumption growth
- **Pricing Changes**: Account for pricing adjustments
- **Market Expansion**: Include market expansion opportunities

#### **Top-Down Forecasting**
- **Total Addressable Market (TAM)**: Size of total market opportunity
- **Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)**: Addressable market with current capabilities
- **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)**: Realistic market share targets
- **Market Growth**: Industry growth rates and trends
- **Competitive Analysis**: Competitive positioning and market share

#### **Scenario Planning**
- **Optimistic Scenario**: 25% probability, high growth assumptions
- **Base Case Scenario**: 50% probability, realistic growth assumptions
- **Conservative Scenario**: 25% probability, conservative growth assumptions
- **Stress Test Scenario**: Extreme market conditions and challenges

### **Budget Planning Process**

#### **Annual Budget Planning**
**Timeline**: Q4 of previous year
**Process**:
1. **Strategic Planning**: Align budget with strategic objectives
2. **Department Budgets**: Each department submits budget requests
3. **Review & Approval**: Management reviews and approves budgets
4. **Consolidation**: Finance consolidates all department budgets
5. **Board Approval**: Board of directors approves final budget

#### **Monthly Budget Reviews**
**Timeline**: First week of each month
**Process**:
1. **Actual vs. Budget**: Compare actual results to budget
2. **Variance Analysis**: Analyze significant variances
3. **Forecast Updates**: Update forecasts based on current trends
4. **Action Plans**: Develop action plans for budget variances
5. **Management Reporting**: Report results to management

#### **Quarterly Budget Revisions**
**Timeline**: End of each quarter
**Process**:
1. **Performance Review**: Review quarterly performance
2. **Market Analysis**: Analyze market conditions and trends
3. **Budget Adjustments**: Make necessary budget adjustments
4. **Strategic Updates**: Update strategic priorities
5. **Communication**: Communicate changes to stakeholders

## ðŸ“ˆ Financial Metrics & KPIs

### **Revenue Metrics**

#### **Growth Metrics**
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Monthly subscription revenue
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**: Annual subscription revenue
- **Revenue Growth Rate**: Month-over-month and year-over-year growth
- **New Revenue**: Revenue from new customers
- **Expansion Revenue**: Revenue from existing customer growth

#### **Customer Metrics**
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Cost to acquire new customers
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Total value of customer relationship
- **CLV/CAC Ratio**: Ratio of customer value to acquisition cost
- **Customer Churn Rate**: Percentage of customers lost
- **Net Revenue Retention**: Net revenue retention rate

#### **Productivity Metrics**
- **Revenue per Employee**: Revenue generated per employee
- **Revenue per Customer**: Average revenue per customer
- **Average Deal Size**: Average size of new deals
- **Sales Cycle Length**: Time from lead to close
- **Win Rate**: Percentage of opportunities won

### **Cost Metrics**

#### **Efficiency Metrics**
- **Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)**: Direct costs of delivering services
- **Gross Margin**: Revenue minus COGS as percentage of revenue
- **Operating Expenses**: Total operating expenses
- **Operating Margin**: Operating income as percentage of revenue
- **Net Margin**: Net income as percentage of revenue

#### **Productivity Metrics**
- **Cost per Employee**: Total costs divided by number of employees
- **Cost per Customer**: Total costs divided by number of customers
- **Cost per Lead**: Marketing costs divided by number of leads
- **Cost per Acquisition**: Total acquisition costs per new customer
- **Cost per Support Ticket**: Support costs divided by number of tickets

### **Cash Flow Metrics**

#### **Cash Flow Metrics**
- **Operating Cash Flow**: Cash generated from operations
- **Free Cash Flow**: Operating cash flow minus capital expenditures
- **Cash Conversion Cycle**: Time to convert investments to cash
- **Cash Runway**: Months of operations with current cash
- **Burn Rate**: Monthly cash consumption rate

#### **Liquidity Metrics**
- **Current Ratio**: Current assets divided by current liabilities
- **Quick Ratio**: Liquid assets divided by current liabilities
- **Cash Ratio**: Cash and equivalents divided by current liabilities
- **Working Capital**: Current assets minus current liabilities
- **Days Sales Outstanding**: Average time to collect receivables
