## ðŸ“‹ **Pitch Checklist**

### Before Sending:
- [ ] Research the publication's style and recent content
- [ ] Personalize the pitch to the specific editor
- [ ] Include relevant credentials and experience
- [ ] Provide a clear, compelling value proposition
- [ ] Attach writing samples or portfolio
- [ ] Specify timeline and exclusivity terms

### After Sending:
- [ ] Follow up within 1-2 weeks if no response
- [ ] Be prepared to provide additional information
- [ ] Have alternative angles ready if needed
- [ ] Maintain professional communication
- [ ] Respect the editor's time and decision

### Success Metrics:
- [ ] Response rate: Target 15-25%
- [ ] Acceptance rate: Target 5-10%
- [ ] Quality of publications secured
- [ ] Relationship building with editors
- [ ] Content performance and engagement
