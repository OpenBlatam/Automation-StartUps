## ðŸŽ¯ **Content Attribution and Tracking**

### Multi-Touch Attribution Models
1. **First-Touch Attribution**
   - **Definition:** Credits the first touchpoint that brought the user
   - **Use Case:** Understanding initial awareness channels
   - **Limitation:** Doesn't account for subsequent touchpoints
   - **Best For:** Awareness campaigns and top-of-funnel content

2. **Last-Touch Attribution**
   - **Definition:** Credits the last touchpoint before conversion
   - **Use Case:** Understanding final conversion drivers
   - **Limitation:** Ignores previous touchpoints
   - **Best For:** Conversion optimization and bottom-of-funnel content

3. **Linear Attribution**
   - **Definition:** Credits all touchpoints equally
   - **Use Case:** Understanding overall content impact
   - **Limitation:** Doesn't weight touchpoints by importance
   - **Best For:** Balanced view of content performance

4. **Time-Decay Attribution**
   - **Definition:** Credits touchpoints based on recency
   - **Use Case:** Understanding recent content impact
   - **Limitation:** May undervalue early touchpoints
   - **Best For:** Short-term campaigns and recent content

5. **Position-Based Attribution**
   - **Definition:** Credits first and last touchpoints more heavily
   - **Use Case:** Understanding awareness and conversion drivers
   - **Limitation:** May undervalue middle touchpoints
   - **Best For:** Full-funnel content analysis
