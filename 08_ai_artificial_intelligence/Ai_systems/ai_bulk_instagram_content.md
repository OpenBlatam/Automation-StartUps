## Instagram Engagement Strategy

### Content Mix:

- **Social Proof (30%):** Testimonials, case studies
- **Behind the Scenes (20%):** Company culture, team

### Engagement Tactics:

- **Create challenges** and trends
- **Respond to comments** quickly

### Posting Schedule:
- **Best times:** 8-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM
- **Frequency:** 1-2 posts per day
- **Stories:** 3-5 per day
- **Reels:** 2-3 per week

## Instagram Analytics and Tracking

### Key Metrics:
- **Reach:** Total unique accounts reached
- **Impressions:** Total number of times content was seen
- **Engagement Rate:** Likes, comments, shares, saves
- **Click-Through Rate:** Link clicks
- **Conversion Rate:** Trial signups

### Performance Tracking:
- **Daily:** Monitor engagement and reach
- **Weekly:** Analyze content performance
- **Monthly:** Review campaign effectiveness
- **Quarterly:** Adjust strategy and budget

### Optimization:
- **A/B Testing:** Different content formats
- **Audience Testing:** Different target groups
- **Creative Testing:** Different visual styles
- **Timing Testing:** Different posting times
