## TikTok Engagement Strategy

### Content Types:
- **Educational (40%):** Tips, tutorials, best practices
- **Entertaining (30%):** Behind the scenes, fun facts
- **Social Proof (20%):** Testimonials, case studies
- **Promotional (10%):** Offers, CTAs, product demos

### Engagement Tactics:
- **Ask questions** in captions
- **Use trending sounds** and music
- **Create challenges** and trends
- **Respond to comments** quickly
- **Collaborate with influencers**

### Posting Schedule:
- **Best times:** 6-9 AM, 12-3 PM, 7-9 PM
- **Frequency:** 3-5 posts per week
- **Consistency:** Same time each day
- **Variety:** Mix of content types

## TikTok Influencer Strategy

### Micro-Influencers (10K-100K followers):
- **Target:** Business and marketing influencers
- **Budget:** $500-1,500 per post
- **Content:** Product demos, reviews, tutorials
- **Engagement:** High engagement rates

### Macro-Influencers (100K-1M followers):
- **Target:** Business and entrepreneurship influencers
- **Budget:** $2,000-5,000 per post
- **Content:** Brand partnerships, sponsored content
- **Reach:** Large audience reach

### Nano-Influencers (1K-10K followers):
- **Target:** Small business owners, entrepreneurs
- **Budget:** $100-500 per post
- **Content:** Authentic testimonials, reviews
- **Trust:** High trust and authenticity
