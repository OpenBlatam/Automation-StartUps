## Troubleshooting Guide

### Common Processing Issues
1. **AI Model Performance Problems**
   - **Issue**: Inconsistent or poor quality output
   - **Solutions**: Refine prompts, adjust parameters, try different models
   - **Prevention**: Regular prompt testing, quality monitoring, model comparison

2. **Bulk Processing Failures**
   - **Issue**: Batch processing errors or timeouts
   - **Solutions**: Reduce batch size, optimize resources, implement retry logic
   - **Prevention**: Resource monitoring, capacity planning, error handling

3. **Quality Control Issues**
   - **Issue**: Inconsistent quality across documents
   - **Solutions**: Standardize processes, enhance review procedures, improve training
   - **Prevention**: Quality checkpoints, standardized templates, regular training

   - **Problem**: Client expectations not met

   - **Prevention**: Detailed requirement gathering, clear communication, regular updates

3. **Quality and Satisfaction Issues**

   - **Prevention**: Quality standards, regular feedback, continuous improvement
