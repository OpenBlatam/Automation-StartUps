## ðŸŽ¯ Sales Process & Methodology

### **Sales Methodology: MEDDIC**

#### **M - Metrics**
**Key Questions**:
- What are your current marketing metrics?
- What metrics are most important to you?
- How do you measure marketing success?
- What are your growth targets?

**Information to Gather**:
- Current campaign performance
- Lead generation metrics
- Conversion rates
- Customer acquisition costs
- Revenue attribution

#### **E - Economic Buyer**
**Key Questions**:
- Who has budget authority for marketing tools?
- Who influences the purchasing decision?
- What is the approval process?
- Who else needs to be involved?

**Stakeholders to Identify**:
- CMO or Marketing Director
- VP of Marketing
- Marketing Operations Manager
- IT Director
- CFO or Finance Director

#### **D - Decision Criteria**
**Key Questions**:
- What are your key requirements?
- What features are must-haves vs. nice-to-haves?
- What are your technical requirements?
- What is your timeline for implementation?

**Criteria to Understand**:
- Feature requirements
- Integration needs
- Security and compliance requirements
- Budget constraints
- Implementation timeline

#### **D - Decision Process**
**Key Questions**:
- What is your decision-making process?
- Who is involved in the evaluation?
- What are the evaluation criteria?
- What is the timeline for decision?

**Process to Map**:
- Evaluation steps
- Decision makers and influencers
- Evaluation criteria and weighting
- Timeline and milestones
- Approval process

#### **I - Identify Pain**
**Key Questions**:
- What are your biggest marketing challenges?
- What is costing you time and money?
- What keeps you up at night?
- What would success look like?

**Pain Points to Identify**:
- Manual processes and inefficiencies
- Lack of integration between tools
- Poor data quality and insights
- Limited personalization capabilities
- Compliance and security concerns

#### **C - Champion**
**Key Questions**:
- Who will be the internal advocate?
- Who will drive the implementation?
- Who will measure success?
- Who will be the day-to-day user?

**Champion Characteristics**:
- Influential within organization
- Experienced with marketing tools
- Motivated to drive change
- Willing to invest time in success

- Discovery call scheduled

- Current state analysis
- Pain point identification

**Success Criteria**:

- Use case scenarios

- ROI clearly demonstrated

### **Objection Handling Scripts**

#### **Objection: "We don't have the budget"**
**Response**:
"I understand budget is always a consideration. Let me ask - what's the cost of not solving [pain point]? 

Many of our customers find that the ROI pays for the investment within the first 3-6 months. For example, [Company X] saw a 40% improvement in campaign performance, which translated to $2M in additional revenue.

Would you be open to exploring a pilot program that could demonstrate the value before making a larger investment?"

#### **Objection: "We're happy with our current solution"**
**Response**:
"I'm glad to hear you're satisfied with your current solution. Many of our customers felt the same way initially.

What I'd like to understand is - are there any areas where you feel your current solution could be better? For example, are you getting the insights you need to make data-driven decisions? Are there any manual processes you'd like to automate?

I'm not suggesting you change everything, but I'd love to show you how we could complement what you're already doing."

#### **Objection: "We need to think about it"**
**Response**:
"Of course, this is an important decision. To help you think through this, let me ask - what specific aspects would you like to consider further?

Is it the technical implementation, the ROI, the timeline, or something else? I'd be happy to provide additional information or connect you with a customer who had similar concerns.

What would help you feel confident about moving forward?"

#### **Objection: "We're too busy to implement something new"**
**Response**:
"I completely understand - everyone is busy these days. That's actually why many companies choose our platform - it's designed to save time, not create more work.

Our implementation process is typically 2-4 weeks, and we handle most of the heavy lifting. Plus, our AI does the work for you once it's set up.

What if I could show you how this would actually reduce your team's workload rather than increase it?"
