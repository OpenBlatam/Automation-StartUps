## üîê Data Protection & Privacy

### **Data Classification & Handling**

#### **Data Classification Levels**
**Public Data**:
- **Definition**: Information that can be freely shared
- **Examples**: Marketing materials, public documentation
- **Controls**: Basic access controls, no encryption required
- **Retention**: Standard retention policies

**Internal Data**:
- **Definition**: Information for internal use only
- **Examples**: Internal policies, procedures, training materials
- **Controls**: Access controls, basic encryption
- **Retention**: Standard retention policies

**Confidential Data**:
- **Definition**: Sensitive business information
- **Examples**: Financial data, business plans, customer lists
- **Controls**: Strong access controls, encryption required
- **Retention**: Extended retention with secure disposal

**Restricted Data**:
- **Definition**: Highly sensitive information
- **Examples**: Personal data, health information, financial records
- **Controls**: Strict access controls, strong encryption, audit logging
- **Retention**: Minimum retention with secure disposal
