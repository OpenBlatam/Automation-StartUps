# Business Continuity Plan
## AI Course, Webinar & SaaS Platform

**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** December 2024  
**Review Date:** March 2025

## Executive Summary

This Business Continuity Plan (BCP) addresses potential disruptions to our AI education business, including:
- AI Course delivery and content management
- Live webinar operations
- SaaS platform for AI marketing tools
- AI bulk document generation services

## Business Impact Analysis

### Critical Business Functions
1. **Course Content Delivery** - Revenue: 40% of total income
2. **Live Webinar Operations** - Revenue: 30% of total income  
3. **SaaS Platform Services** - Revenue: 25% of total income
4. **AI Bulk Document Generation** - Revenue: 5% of total income

### Maximum Tolerable Downtime (MTD)
- **Course Platform**: 4 hours
- **Webinar Services**: 1 hour
- **SaaS Platform**: 2 hours
- **AI Bulk Services**: 6 hours

## Disruption Scenarios & Response Frameworks

### Scenario 1: IT Infrastructure Outage

**Probability:** High | **Impact:** Critical

#### Immediate Response (0-30 minutes)
- **Trigger:** System monitoring alerts or user reports
- **Communication Protocol:**
  - Notify all stakeholders via Slack/Teams
  - Send automated email to all users
  - Post status update on website and social media
- **Leadership Responsibility:** CTO + Operations Manager

#### Short-term Response (30 minutes - 4 hours)
- **Backup Systems Activation:**
  - Switch to backup servers (AWS/GCP)
  - Activate CDN failover
  - Enable mobile app offline mode
- **Recovery Timeline:** 2-4 hours
- **Communication:** Hourly updates to users

#### Long-term Response (4+ hours)
- **Alternative Solutions:**
  - Migrate to cloud backup infrastructure
  - Implement manual processes for critical functions
  - Activate emergency support channels

---

### Scenario 2: Supply Chain Failure (Third-party Services)

**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High

#### Affected Services:
- Video hosting (Vimeo/YouTube)
- Payment processing (Stripe/PayPal)
- Email services (Mailchimp/SendGrid)
- AI API providers (OpenAI/Anthropic)

#### Response Framework:
1. **Immediate (0-1 hour):**
   - Assess impact scope
   - Activate backup service providers
   - Notify customers of potential delays

2. **Short-term (1-24 hours):**
   - Implement alternative payment methods
   - Switch to backup video hosting
   - Use alternative AI providers

3. **Recovery Timeline:** 4-24 hours
4. **Leadership:** CEO + Technical Lead

---

### Scenario 3: Health Crisis (Pandemic/Staff Illness)

**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium-High

#### Response Framework:
1. **Remote Work Activation:**
   - All staff transition to remote work
   - VPN and security protocols activated
   - Daily check-ins established

2. **Service Continuity:**
   - Pre-recorded content as backup
   - Automated webinar systems
   - Self-service customer support

3. **Recovery Timeline:** Immediate transition
4. **Leadership:** HR Manager + Operations

---

### Scenario 4: Cybersecurity Incident

**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** Critical

#### Immediate Response (0-1 hour):
- **Incident Response Team Activation**
- **System Isolation**
- **Stakeholder Notification**

#### Short-term Response (1-24 hours):
- **Forensic Analysis**
- **System Restoration**
- **Security Hardening**

#### Recovery Timeline:** 24-72 hours
**Leadership:** CTO + Security Officer
