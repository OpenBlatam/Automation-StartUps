- **Size:** 150 employees

  - Real-time regulatory change alerts

  - Risk assessment and scoring
  - Audit-ready reporting

- **Data Sources:** SEC EDGAR, EU Official Journal, UK FCA, internal policies
- **Processing Volume:** 2,000+ documents per month
- **Alert System:** 3-tier alert system (Critical, Warning, Info)
- **Integration:** Connected to existing legal workflow tools
