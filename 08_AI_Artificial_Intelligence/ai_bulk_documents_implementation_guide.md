1. **Registration**
   - Visit [www.aibulkdocs.com](https://www.aibulkdocs.com)
   - Click "Sign Up" and create your account
   - Verify your email address
   - Complete your profile information

2. **Subscription Selection**
   - Choose from our flexible pricing plans
   - Select your preferred billing cycle
   - Add payment information
   - Confirm your subscription

3. **Initial Configuration**
   - Set up your organization profile
   - Configure document templates
   - Define your brand guidelines
   - Set up user permissions

### **Step 2: First Document Creation**
1. **Access the Dashboard**
   - Log into your account
   - Navigate to the main dashboard
   - Click "Create New Document"

2. **Enter Your Query**
   - Type your document request in natural language
   - Be specific about document type and requirements
   - Include any special formatting or style preferences
   - Add context about your audience and purpose

3. **Generate Your Document**
   - Click "Generate Document"
   - Wait for AI processing (typically 2-5 seconds)
   - Review the generated content
   - Make any necessary adjustments

4. **Download and Use**
   - Export in your preferred format (PDF, Word, HTML)
   - Save to your document library
   - Share with team members
   - Integrate into your workflow
