### üß™ Tweet Evaluation Framework

#### Hook Strength (1-10)
- **10**: Stops scroll immediately, creates instant curiosity
- **8-9**: Strong emotional or intellectual hook
- **6-7**: Decent attention-grabber
- **Below 6**: Rewrite immediately

#### Clarity (1-10)
- **10**: Crystal clear message in one read
- **8-9**: Easy to understand, minimal cognitive load
- **6-7**: Some confusion possible
- **Below 6**: Simplify language and structure

#### Value Density (1-10)
- **10**: Every word serves a purpose
- **8-9**: High information-to-word ratio
- **6-7**: Some filler words present
- **Below 6**: Cut unnecessary words

#### Emotional Resonance (1-10)
- **10**: Triggers strong emotional response
- **8-9**: Creates curiosity or reflection
- **6-7**: Mild emotional engagement
- **Below 6**: Add emotional triggers

#### Shareability (1-10)
- **10**: Highly quotable, save-worthy
- **8-9**: Good retweet potential
- **6-7**: Some share value
- **Below 6**: Add quotable elements

#### Conversion Potential (1-10)
- **10**: Natural path to profile visit/action
- **8-9**: Clear engagement opportunity
- **6-7**: Some conversion potential
- **Below 6**: Strengthen CTA
