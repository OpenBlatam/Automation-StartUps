## Employee Resource Groups (ERGs) Introduction

### What are Employee Resource Groups?
Employee Resource Groups are voluntary, employee-led groups that foster a diverse, inclusive workplace aligned with our organizational mission, values, goals, business practices, and objectives.

### Our AI-Focused ERGs

#### 1. AI Innovation Group
**Mission**: Drive AI innovation and adoption across the organization
- Monthly AI tool demonstrations
- Innovation challenges and hackathons
- Cross-departmental AI project collaboration
- **Meeting Schedule**: First Tuesday of each month, 4:00 PM

#### 2. AI Ethics & Governance Committee
**Mission**: Ensure responsible AI implementation and ethical considerations
- AI ethics training and workshops
- Policy development and review
- Ethical AI case study discussions
- **Meeting Schedule**: Third Thursday of each month, 2:00 PM

#### 3. AI Learning & Development Network
**Mission**: Support continuous learning and skill development in AI
- Study groups and peer learning sessions
- Resource sharing and knowledge exchange
- Mentorship programs for AI newcomers
- **Meeting Schedule**: Every other Wednesday, 12:00 PM

#### 4. AI Diversity & Inclusion Alliance
**Mission**: Promote diversity and inclusion in AI development and usage
- Bias detection and mitigation workshops
- Inclusive AI design principles
- Community outreach and education
- **Meeting Schedule**: Last Friday of each month, 3:00 PM

### How to Join an ERG
1. **Attend a Meeting**: Join any ERG meeting as a guest to learn more
2. **Express Interest**: Contact the ERG leader or HR department
3. **Complete Application**: Fill out the ERG membership form
4. **Get Involved**: Participate in activities and contribute to the group's mission

### Benefits of Joining ERGs
- **Professional Development**: Enhance your AI skills and knowledge
- **Networking**: Connect with like-minded colleagues
- **Leadership Opportunities**: Take on leadership roles within ERGs
- **Influence**: Help shape AI policies and practices
- **Community**: Be part of a supportive learning community
