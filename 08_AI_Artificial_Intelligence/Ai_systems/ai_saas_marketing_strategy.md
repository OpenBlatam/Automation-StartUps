### üß™ Tweet Evaluation Framework

#### Hook Strength (1-10)
- **10**: Stops scroll immediately with compelling contrast
- **8-9**: Strong emotional or intellectual hook
- **6-7**: Decent attention-grabber
- **Below 6**: Rewrite with stronger contrast or surprise

#### Clarity (1-10)
- **10**: Crystal clear value proposition in one read
- **8-9**: Easy to understand benefits
- **6-7**: Some confusion possible
- **Below 6**: Simplify language and structure

#### Value Density (1-10)
- **10**: Every word drives toward demo/trial signup
- **8-9**: High conversion potential
- **6-7**: Some filler content
- **Below 6**: Focus on core benefits

#### Emotional Resonance (1-10)
- **10**: Triggers FOMO, excitement, or fear of being left behind
- **8-9**: Creates strong emotional response
- **6-7**: Mild emotional engagement
- **Below 6**: Add emotional triggers

#### Shareability (1-10)
- **10**: Highly shareable with clear value
- **8-9**: Good retweet potential
- **6-7**: Some share value
- **Below 6**: Add quotable elements

#### Conversion Potential (1-10)
- **10**: Direct path to demo request or trial signup
- **8-9**: Clear conversion opportunity
- **6-7**: Some conversion potential
- **Below 6**: Strengthen CTA and value proposition
