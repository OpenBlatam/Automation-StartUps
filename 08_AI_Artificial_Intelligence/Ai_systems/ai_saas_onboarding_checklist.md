# New Hire Onboarding Checklist
## AI SaaS Company - Welcome to the Future of AI-Powered Marketing

---

## üéØ Onboarding Overview

**Duration**: 90 days (3 months)
**Format**: Structured program with milestones and checkpoints
**Support**: Dedicated onboarding buddy + HR support
**Goal**: Seamless integration into our AI SaaS team and culture

## üìã Pre-Start Checklist (HR & Manager)

### **Week -2: Pre-Boarding Preparation**
- [ ] **HR Tasks**
  - [ ] Send welcome email with first-day details
  - [ ] Prepare employee ID and access cards
  - [ ] Set up payroll and benefits enrollment
  - [ ] Order business cards and company swag
  - [ ] Schedule IT equipment setup
  - [ ] Assign onboarding buddy
  - [ ] Prepare workspace and equipment

- [ ] **Manager Tasks**
  - [ ] Review job description and expectations
  - [ ] Prepare 30-60-90 day goals
  - [ ] Schedule team introduction meetings
  - [ ] Plan first week activities
  - [ ] Prepare project assignments
  - [ ] Review company culture and values
  - [ ] Set up regular 1:1 meeting schedule

- [ ] **IT Tasks**
  - [ ] Create user accounts and email
  - [ ] Set up laptop/computer with required software
  - [ ] Configure access to company systems
  - [ ] Install development tools and environments
  - [ ] Set up VPN and security protocols
  - [ ] Prepare phone and communication tools

## üöÄ Day 1: Welcome & Orientation

### **Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)**
- [ ] **Welcome & Introductions** (9:00 AM - 9:30 AM)
  - [ ] HR welcome and company overview
  - [ ] Meet direct manager
  - [ ] Introduction to onboarding buddy
  - [ ] Office tour and workspace setup
  - [ ] Company swag and welcome package

- [ ] **IT Setup & Access** (9:30 AM - 10:30 AM)
  - [ ] Laptop/computer setup and configuration
  - [ ] Email and communication tools setup
  - [ ] Access to company systems and platforms
  - [ ] Password management and security protocols
  - [ ] VPN and remote access setup

- [ ] **Company Culture & Values** (10:30 AM - 11:30 AM)
  - [ ] Company mission, vision, and values presentation
  - [ ] Culture and traditions overview
  - [ ] Employee resource groups introduction
  - [ ] Diversity and inclusion initiatives
  - [ ] Code of conduct and ethics training

- [ ] **Benefits & Policies** (11:30 AM - 12:00 PM)
  - [ ] Benefits enrollment and overview
  - [ ] Health insurance and wellness programs
  - [ ] PTO and leave policies
  - [ ] Remote work and flexible arrangements
  - [ ] Performance review process

### **Afternoon (1:00 PM - 5:00 PM)**
- [ ] **Team Introductions** (1:00 PM - 2:00 PM)
  - [ ] Meet immediate team members
  - [ ] Department overview and structure
  - [ ] Role and responsibility clarification
  - [ ] Team communication preferences
  - [ ] Collaboration tools and processes

- [ ] **Product & Technology Overview** (2:00 PM - 3:00 PM)
  - [ ] AI SaaS platform demonstration
  - [ ] Technology stack and architecture
  - [ ] Development processes and workflows
  - [ ] Quality assurance and testing procedures
  - [ ] Security and compliance requirements

- [ ] **First Project Assignment** (3:00 PM - 4:00 PM)
  - [ ] Review first week tasks and goals
  - [ ] Access to project management tools
  - [ ] Documentation and resource access
  - [ ] Training materials and learning paths
  - [ ] Support and mentorship setup

- [ ] **Wrap-up & Questions** (4:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
  - [ ] Day 1 reflection and feedback
  - [ ] Questions and concerns discussion
  - [ ] Next steps and week 1 planning
  - [ ] Buddy check-in and support
  - [ ] Schedule follow-up meetings
