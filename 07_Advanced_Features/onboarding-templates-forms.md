```
# First Week Onboarding Checklist

## Day 1: Welcome & Introduction
- [ ] Welcome meeting with HR and direct manager
- [ ] Complete remaining paperwork and documentation
- [ ] Office tour and workspace setup
- [ ] Introduction to team members and key stakeholders
- [ ] Company history, mission, and values presentation
- [ ] Review of employee handbook and AI ethics guidelines
- [ ] Lunch with team or manager
- [ ] End-of-day check-in and Q&A session
- [ ] AI ethics workshop and responsible development principles
- [ ] Technical deep-dive on AI/ML frameworks and company tech stack

- [ ] Product overview (AI course, webinar platform, SaaS tools)
- [ ] Customer journey and target audience analysis
- [ ] Competitive landscape and market positioning
- [ ] Revenue model and key business metrics
- [ ] Technology stack and development processes
- [ ] Marketing and sales strategies
- [ ] AI model performance and capabilities demo
- [ ] Customer success stories and ROI examples
- [ ] Data privacy and AI ethics framework
- [ ] Future AI roadmap and innovation pipeline

## Day 4-5: Process & Tools Training
- [ ] Project management tools training
- [ ] Communication protocols and meeting cadences
- [ ] Documentation standards and knowledge base
- [ ] Quality assurance and review processes
- [ ] Customer support and escalation procedures
- [ ] Security and compliance training
- [ ] Performance review and feedback processes
- [ ] Career development and growth opportunities
- [ ] AI model testing and validation processes
- [ ] Data handling and privacy protocols
- [ ] AI bias detection and mitigation procedures
- [ ] Customer feedback and model improvement workflows

- [ ] CEO/Founder Session: Vision, strategy, and company culture

- [ ] Customer Success Stories: Real examples of impact and results
- [ ] Team Building: Informal activities to build relationships
- [ ] AI Ethics Workshop: Responsible AI development principles
- [ ] Technical Deep-Dive: AI/ML frameworks and company tech stack
- [ ] Customer Journey Mapping: Understanding user experience
- [ ] Innovation Lab Tour: See AI development in action
```
