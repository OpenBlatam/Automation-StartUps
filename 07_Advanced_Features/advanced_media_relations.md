```

   - Crisis severity and impact assessment
   - Stakeholder identification and prioritization
   - Response strategy development

2. Immediate Response (0-2 hours)
   - Crisis response team activation
   - Initial communication and statements
   - Media notification and briefing
   - Stakeholder communication and updates

3. Sustained Response (2-48 hours)
   - Detailed crisis communication strategy
   - Media engagement and management
   - Stakeholder communication and updates
   - Reputation protection and management

4. Recovery & Positioning (48+ hours)
   - Crisis resolution and closure
   - Reputation recovery and rebuilding
   - Strategic positioning and messaging
   - Long-term relationship rebuilding
```

**Crisis Communication Templates:**
```
Crisis Response Templates:
1. Immediate Response Statement
"We are aware of [situation] and are taking immediate action to [response]. 
Our priority is [key message]. We will provide updates as we have more information."

2. Detailed Crisis Statement
"We have been made aware of [situation] and are conducting a thorough investigation. 
Our immediate priority is [primary concern]. We are working with [authorities/experts] 
to [action taken]. We will provide regular updates as we learn more."

3. Recovery and Moving Forward
"We have completed our investigation into [situation] and have implemented 
[corrective actions]. We have learned valuable lessons and are committed to 
[improvements]. We will continue to [ongoing actions] and provide updates."
```
