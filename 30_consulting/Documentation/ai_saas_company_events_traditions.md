## üìÖ Annual Company Events

### 1. AI Innovation Summit (March)
**What**: Our flagship annual conference showcasing the latest in AI marketing technology
- **Duration**: 2 days
- **Location**: Company headquarters + virtual streaming
- **Activities**: 
  - Keynote presentations from industry leaders
  - Product demonstrations and beta releases
  - AI hackathon competition
  - Networking with clients and partners
  - Awards ceremony for innovation achievements

### 2. Summer Tech Retreat (July)
**What**: A fun-filled weekend combining team building with technology exploration
- **Duration**: 3 days, 2 nights
- **Location**: Mountain resort (varies annually)
- **Activities**:
  - Outdoor team challenges
  - AI workshop sessions
  - Product roadmap planning
  - Bonfire discussions about the future of AI
  - Talent show featuring tech demos

### 3. Customer Success Celebration (October)
**What**: Recognizing our clients' achievements and our team's contributions
- **Duration**: 1 day
- **Location**: Convention center
- **Activities**:
  - Customer success story presentations
  - Product roadmap reveals
  - Client appreciation awards
  - Interactive AI demo stations
  - Gala dinner and entertainment

### 4. Year-End AI Awards Gala (December)
**What**: Celebrating our team's accomplishments and looking forward to the new year
- **Duration**: Evening event
- **Location**: Upscale venue
- **Activities**:
  - Employee recognition awards
  - AI innovation showcase
  - Year-in-review presentation
  - Holiday party with live entertainment
  - Secret Santa gift exchange
