## ðŸ“ˆ Customer Health Monitoring

### **Health Score Calculation**

#### **Usage Metrics (40% weight)**
- **Login Frequency**: Daily, weekly, monthly active users
- **Feature Adoption**: Percentage of available features used
- **Campaign Activity**: Number of active campaigns
- **Data Volume**: Amount of data processed and analyzed
- **Integration Usage**: Usage of connected integrations

#### **Performance Metrics (30% weight)**
- **Campaign Performance**: Open rates, click rates, conversion rates
- **ROI Achievement**: Return on investment from campaigns
- **Goal Achievement**: Progress toward defined success metrics
- **Efficiency Gains**: Time and cost savings achieved
- **Business Impact**: Measurable business outcomes

#### **Engagement Metrics (20% weight)**
- **Support Interactions**: Frequency and quality of support interactions
- **Training Participation**: Participation in training and education
- **Community Engagement**: Participation in user community
- **Feedback Quality**: Quality and frequency of feedback
- **Relationship Strength**: Strength of relationship with CS team

#### **Risk Indicators (10% weight)**
- **Support Ticket Volume**: Number and severity of support tickets
- **Feature Requests**: Number of feature requests and complaints
- **Contract Renewal Risk**: Risk of non-renewal
- **Competitive Threats**: Threats from competitors
- **Business Changes**: Changes in customer's business

### **Health Score Ranges**

#### **Healthy (80-100)**
- **Characteristics**: High usage, strong performance, active engagement
- **Actions**: Maintain current engagement, identify expansion opportunities
- **Frequency**: Monthly check-ins, quarterly business reviews
- **Focus**: Growth and optimization

#### **At Risk (60-79)**
- **Characteristics**: Declining usage, performance issues, reduced engagement
- **Actions**: Proactive outreach, issue resolution, re-engagement
- **Frequency**: Bi-weekly check-ins, monthly business reviews
- **Focus**: Issue resolution and re-engagement

#### **Critical (0-59)**
- **Characteristics**: Very low usage, poor performance, minimal engagement
- **Actions**: Immediate intervention, executive engagement, retention efforts
- **Frequency**: Weekly check-ins, bi-weekly business reviews
- **Focus**: Retention and recovery
