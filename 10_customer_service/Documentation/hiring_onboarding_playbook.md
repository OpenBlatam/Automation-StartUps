# Hiring & Onboarding Playbook: AI Business Scaling

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive hiring and onboarding playbook provides detailed processes, templates, and best practices for scaling your AI business team from startup to market leader. The playbook covers recruitment strategies, interview processes, onboarding procedures, and retention strategies for all phases of growth.

## Hiring Strategy Overview

### Team Scaling Timeline
```
Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Core Team (5-8 people)
- Technical Lead
- Customer Success Manager
- Content Marketing Specialist
- Sales Development Representative
- AI Engineer (Month 3-4)

Phase 2 (Months 6-18): Growth Team (15-25 people)
- VP of Sales
- Head of Product
- AI Research Scientist
- International Business Development
- DevOps Engineer
- UX/UI Designer
- Data Analyst
- Additional Customer Success
- Additional Sales Reps

Phase 3 (Months 18-36): Scale Team (30-50 people)
- C-level executives
- Department heads
- Regional managers
- Specialized roles
- Support staff
```

### Hiring Philosophy
- **Quality over Quantity**: Hire the best talent, not just bodies
- **Cultural Fit**: Skills can be taught, culture fit is essential
- **Diversity and Inclusion**: Build a diverse, inclusive team
- **Remote-First**: Leverage global talent pool
- **Growth Mindset**: Hire people who can grow with the company

## Phase 1: Core Team Hiring (Months 1-6)

### Priority 1: Technical Lead

#### Job Description
```
**Position**: Technical Lead - AI Platform Development
**Location**: Remote (Latin America preferred)
**Salary Range**: $8,000 - $12,000/month
**Equity**: 0.5% - 1.0%
**Reports to**: CTO/Founder

**Role Overview**:
Lead the development of AI-powered platforms across three verticals: course platform, marketing SaaS, and document generation service. This role is critical for building the technical foundation that will scale the business.

**Key Responsibilities**:
- Architect and develop scalable AI platforms
- Lead technical team and development processes
- Integrate AI/ML capabilities into existing systems
- Ensure system performance, security, and reliability
- Mentor and develop technical team members
- Collaborate with product and business teams

**Required Skills**:
- 5+ years full-stack development experience
- 3+ years AI/ML implementation experience
- Proficiency in Python, JavaScript, React, Node.js
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS/GCP)
- Knowledge of AI/ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- Experience with APIs and microservices architecture
- Strong problem-solving and communication skills

**Preferred Qualifications**:
- Bilingual (Spanish/English)
- Experience with OpenAI API or similar services
- Previous startup experience
- Leadership experience
- Experience with educational technology
- SaaS platform development experience

**Success Metrics**:
- System uptime: 99.9%+
- Development velocity: 2x improvement
- Team productivity: 50% increase
- Customer satisfaction: 90%+
- Technical debt reduction: 30%
```

#### Interview Process
```
**Step 1: Initial Screening (30 minutes)**
- Phone/video call with HR/Founder
- Basic qualifications and interest assessment
- Cultural fit preliminary evaluation
- Salary and expectations discussion

**Step 2: Technical Assessment (2 hours)**
- Live coding session (1 hour)
- System design interview (45 minutes)
- AI/ML knowledge assessment (15 minutes)
- Problem-solving approach evaluation

**Step 3: Technical Deep Dive (1.5 hours)**
- Architecture design challenge
- Code review and best practices
- Performance optimization discussion
- Security and scalability considerations

**Step 4: Cultural Fit Interview (45 minutes)**
- Team collaboration assessment
- Leadership style evaluation
- Growth mindset evaluation
- Communication skills assessment

**Step 5: Final Interview (1 hour)**
- Meeting with CTO/Founder
- Role expectations and growth path
- Company vision and mission alignment
- Final questions and decision

**Decision Timeline**: 1 week from final interview
```


Key success factors:
