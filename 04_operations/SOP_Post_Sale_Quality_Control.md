# SOP: Post‑Sale Quality Control

Code: QA-POST-001  
Version: 1.0  
Date: 2025-10-30

---

### 1. Objective
Standardize post‑sale quality control to ensure satisfaction, reduce returns/claims, and drive continuous improvement.

### 2. Scope
- Applies to all closed orders and service deliveries.  
- Includes compliance verification, surveys, ticket audits, and feedback to Ops/Product.

### 3. Roles & Responsibilities
- Sponsor: Director of Quality/Operations  
- Owner: Quality Lead  
- Support: Customer Support, Operations, Product, Data

#### 3.1 RACI (summary)
- Delivery checklist: R Ops, A Quality Lead, C Support, I Sales  
- Post‑sale survey (CSAT/NPS): R Support, A Quality Lead, C Data, I Ops  
- Ticket audit: R Quality, A Dir. Ops, C Support, I Product  
- Corrective actions: R Ops/Product, A Area Director, C Quality, I Support

### 4. Process
#### 4.1 Delivery Verification (Day 0–2)
1) Compliance checklist (specs, docs, SLAs)  
2) Customer receipt confirmation  
3) Evidence (photos/docs) if applicable

#### 4.2 Post‑Sale Survey (Day 2–7)
1) Send CSAT (0–5) and NPS (−100 to 100)  
2) Follow up detractors (SLA ≤ 48 h)  
3) Classify reasons and update knowledge base

#### 4.3 Ticket Audit (Weekly)
1) Stratified sampling of closed tickets  
2) QA rubric (time, resolution, tone, compliance)  
3) Corrective actions and training

#### 4.4 Feedback to Ops/Product (Monthly)
1) Defect report and root cause (Pareto)  
2) Prioritize improvements (ICE/RICE)  
3) Track effectiveness (pre vs post change)

### 5. Tools
Helpdesk/CRM, Survey (Typeform/Qualtrics), BI, Quality manager.

### 6. SLAs
Survey sent ≤ 72 h; detractor contact ≤ 48 h; critical corrective actions ≤ 7 days; weekly audit.

### 7. KPIs
CSAT/NPS, response rate, claims/returns rate, time to resolution, FCR, defect rate, churn/upsell impact.

### 8. Quality & Controls
Documented checklists/audits, action traceability, monthly compliance and sampling bias audit.

### 9. Reviews
Weekly critical claims; Monthly trends and improvements; Quarterly program review.
