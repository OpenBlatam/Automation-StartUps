### **Customer Support Procedures**

#### **Support Ticket Management**
1. **Ticket Creation**: Customer creates support ticket
2. **Initial Response**: Acknowledge ticket within 2 hours
3. **Triage**: Categorize and prioritize ticket
4. **Assignment**: Assign to appropriate team member
5. **Resolution**: Work to resolve issue
6. **Follow-up**: Follow up to ensure satisfaction
7. **Closure**: Close ticket and document resolution

#### **Support Levels**
- **Level 1**: Basic questions and account issues
- **Level 2**: Technical issues and troubleshooting
- **Level 3**: Complex technical problems
- **Level 4**: Escalation to engineering team

#### **Response Time SLAs**
- **Critical**: 1 hour (system down, security issues)
- **High**: 4 hours (major functionality issues)
- **Medium**: 24 hours (general support questions)
- **Low**: 72 hours (feature requests, general inquiries)

#### **Support Channels**
- **Email**: support@aimarketingsolutions.com
- **Live Chat**: Available during business hours
- **Phone**: Emergency support for enterprise customers
- **Knowledge Base**: Self-service resources and documentation
- **Community Forum**: Peer-to-peer support and discussions
