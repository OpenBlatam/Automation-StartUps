# SOP: VIP Client Management

Code: CX-VIP-001  
Version: 1.0  
Date: 2025-10-30

---

### 1. Objective
Deliver a superior, proactive and measurable experience for VIP clients to increase retention, expansion (upsell/cross-sell), and advocacy with prioritized support.

### 2. Scope
- Applies to accounts classified as VIP by revenue, potential, influence, or operational criticality.
- Covers onboarding, ongoing relationship, support, account health, success plans, QBR/EBR and renewals.

### 3. Roles & Responsibilities
- Sponsor: Director of Customer Success  
- Owner: Senior CSM  
- Support: L2 Support, Solutions Architect, Sales, Finance, Legal

#### 3.1 RACI (summary)
- VIP Classification: R (RevOps), A (Dir. CS), C (Sales), I (Finance)  
- Success Plan: R (CSM), A (Dir. CS), C (Client/Architect), I (Sales)  
- QBR/EBR: R (CSM), A (Dir. CS), C (Data/Product), I (Client Exec)  
- Renewal: R (CSM), A (Sales/Dir. CS), C (Finance/Legal), I (Ops)

### 4. Process
#### 4.1 Identification & Onboarding (Week 0–2)
1) Confirm VIP criteria and assign senior CSM  
2) Kickoff with stakeholder map and goals  
3) Success Plan (quarterly goals, milestones, owners, risks)  
4) Executive reporting and dedicated channel (Slack/Teams)

#### 4.2 Ongoing Operations (Weekly/Monthly)
1) Health monitoring (adoption, NPS, tickets, realized value)  
2) Bi‑weekly/monthly meeting (agenda, minutes, actions)  
3) Risk log with owners and mitigations  
4) Joint roadmap and feedback loop  
5) Escalations via L2/L3 playbook with SLAs

#### 4.3 QBR/EBR (Quarterly)
1) Goals vs results (business KPIs)  
2) ROI/value delivered, success cases and benchmarks  
3) Risks/impediments and plan  
4) Roadmap and opportunities (upsell/cross‑sell)  
5) Approval of next account OKRs

#### 4.4 Renewal & Expansion (90–60–30 days)
Health check and renewal forecast; proposal; negotiation with Sales/Finance/Legal; closing and comms.

### 5. Tools
CRM/CS platform, BI dashboards, Slack/Teams, ClickUp/Jira.

### 6. SLAs
Response during business hours ≤ 30 min; after hours ≤ 2 h; L2 ≤ 4 h; L3 ≤ 24 h; QBR quarterly.

### 7. KPIs
NRR, logo retention, Health Score, response/resolution time, upsell/cross‑sell rate, NPS/CSAT.

### 8. Quality & Controls
QBR checklist, monthly health/risk review, quarterly SLA/doc audit, post‑interaction pulse surveys.

### 9. Reviews
Weekly: at‑risk accounts; Monthly: VIP performance; Quarterly: program audit and improvements.
