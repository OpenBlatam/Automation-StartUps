- **User Authentication**: Multi-factor authentication required
- **Access Logging**: All document access tracked and logged
- **Time Limits**: Documents expire after 30 days
- **Download Restrictions**: No downloads without approval
- **Print Restrictions**: No printing of confidential documents

### **Document Security**
- **Watermarking**: All documents watermarked with user information
- **Encryption**: All documents encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of document access
- **Audit Trail**: Complete audit trail of all activities

### **User Management**
- **Role-Based Access**: Different access levels for different roles
- **User Onboarding**: New users require approval and training
- **User Offboarding**: Access revoked immediately upon departure
- **Regular Reviews**: Quarterly access reviews and updates
