The ClickUp Brain Cybersecurity Framework provides a comprehensive approach to cybersecurity, enabling organizations to protect their digital assets, ensure data privacy, and maintain business continuity through robust security measures and risk management. This framework integrates cybersecurity strategy, security architecture, threat management, compliance management, and security operations to create a robust cybersecurity system.
