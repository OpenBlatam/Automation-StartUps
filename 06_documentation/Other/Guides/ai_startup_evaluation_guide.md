# AI Startup Evaluation Guide
## Sector-Specific Criteria for AI Investments

### Key AI Evaluation Dimensions

#### 1. Technical Differentiation (Critical)
**AI Model Performance**
- Accuracy metrics vs. benchmarks
- Model efficiency (inference speed, memory usage)
- Novel architectures or approaches
- Research publications and citations

**Data Advantage**
- Proprietary datasets (size, quality, uniqueness)
- Data collection methods and sources
- Data labeling and annotation processes
- Data moat sustainability

**Technical Infrastructure**
- Scalability of AI systems
- Cloud vs. edge deployment capabilities
- Model training and inference infrastructure
- API performance and reliability

#### 2. Market Application (Critical)
**Problem-Solution Fit**
- Clear AI use case with measurable ROI
- Quantifiable business impact
- Customer pain point severity
- Market readiness for AI adoption

**Competitive Landscape**
- Incumbent solutions and their limitations
- AI-native vs. AI-enhanced approaches
- Open source alternatives
- Patent landscape and IP protection

#### 3. Business Model (Important)
**Revenue Model**
- SaaS vs. API vs. embedded licensing
- Usage-based vs. seat-based pricing
- Enterprise vs. SMB focus
- Recurring revenue characteristics

**Unit Economics**
- Cost per inference/prediction
- Customer acquisition costs
- Lifetime value calculations
- Gross margin sustainability

#### 4. Team & Execution (Critical)
**AI Talent**
- PhD researchers and ML engineers
- Previous AI/ML experience
- Publications and patents
- Technical advisory board

**Product Development**
- MVP to production timeline
- Model iteration and improvement cycles
- Customer feedback integration
- Technical roadmap execution

### AI-Specific Red Flags

#### Technical Red Flags
- No proprietary data or weak data moat
- Over-reliance on third-party models
- Poor model performance vs. benchmarks
- Unsustainable compute costs
- No clear technical differentiation

#### Market Red Flags
- Vague AI use cases without clear ROI
- Overhyped AI claims without substance
- Regulatory uncertainty (AI governance)
- Long enterprise sales cycles
- Commoditized AI capabilities

#### Business Red Flags
- Poor unit economics (high compute costs)
- Unclear monetization strategy
- High customer acquisition costs
- Limited scalability potential
- Dependency on single customers

### AI Investment Checklist

#### Technical Due Diligence
- [ ] Model performance benchmarks
- [ ] Data quality and sources
- [ ] Infrastructure scalability
- [ ] IP portfolio review
- [ ] Technical team assessment
- [ ] Code and architecture review

#### Market Validation
- [ ] Customer reference calls
- [ ] Competitive analysis
- [ ] Market size validation
- [ ] Adoption barriers assessment
- [ ] Regulatory compliance check

#### Business Validation
- [ ] Unit economics modeling
- [ ] Revenue projections
- [ ] Customer concentration analysis
- [ ] Go-to-market strategy
- [ ] Partnership opportunities

### AI Sector Benchmarks

#### Early Stage (Pre-Seed/Seed)
- **Team**: 2-5 people, strong technical founders
- **Traction**: 0-10 customers, early pilots
- **Revenue**: $0-50K MRR
- **Valuation**: $5-20M pre-money

#### Growth Stage (Series A)
- **Team**: 10-25 people, complete founding team
- **Traction**: 50-200 customers, product-market fit
- **Revenue**: $100K-1M MRR
- **Valuation**: $20-100M pre-money

#### Scale Stage (Series B)
- **Team**: 25-100 people, experienced management
- **Traction**: 200+ customers, strong growth
- **Revenue**: $1M+ MRR
- **Valuation**: $100M+ pre-money

### Key Questions for AI Startups

#### Technical Questions
1. What makes your AI model unique?
2. How do you ensure model accuracy and reliability?
3. What's your data strategy and moat?
4. How do you handle model bias and fairness?
5. What's your approach to model updates and versioning?

#### Business Questions
1. What's the ROI for your customers?
2. How do you price your AI services?
3. What are your customer acquisition costs?
4. How do you handle enterprise sales cycles?
5. What's your competitive differentiation?

#### Market Questions
1. How large is your addressable market?
2. What's the adoption timeline for your solution?
3. How do you handle regulatory compliance?
4. What partnerships are critical to your success?
5. How do you scale your go-to-market strategy?
