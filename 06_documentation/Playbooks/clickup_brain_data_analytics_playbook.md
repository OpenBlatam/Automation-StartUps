## üìä Advanced Analytics Techniques

### Descriptive Analytics

#### 1. Statistical Analysis
- **Descriptive Statistics**: Mean, median, mode, standard deviation
- **Distribution Analysis**: Normal, skewed, bimodal distributions
- **Correlation Analysis**: Pearson, Spearman correlation coefficients
- **Trend Analysis**: Time series analysis and trend identification

#### 2. Data Exploration
- **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**: Comprehensive data exploration
- **Data Profiling**: Understanding data characteristics and quality
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identifying patterns and anomalies
- **Segmentation Analysis**: Customer and market segmentation

#### 3. Reporting & Dashboards
- **Executive Dashboards**: High-level business metrics
- **Operational Dashboards**: Day-to-day operational metrics
- **Performance Scorecards**: Balanced scorecard approach
- **Ad-hoc Reporting**: Flexible and customizable reports

### Diagnostic Analytics

#### 1. Root Cause Analysis
- **Drill-Down Analysis**: Deep dive into specific issues
- **Comparative Analysis**: Compare performance across periods/segments
- **Variance Analysis**: Analyze deviations from targets
- **Attribution Analysis**: Identify factors contributing to outcomes

#### 2. Statistical Testing
- **Hypothesis Testing**: Test business hypotheses with data
- **A/B Testing**: Compare different approaches or treatments
- **Chi-Square Tests**: Test relationships between categorical variables
- **Regression Analysis**: Understand relationships between variables

#### 3. Data Mining
- **Association Rules**: Find relationships between items
- **Clustering**: Group similar data points
- **Classification**: Predict categorical outcomes
- **Anomaly Detection**: Identify unusual patterns or outliers

### Predictive Analytics

#### 1. Forecasting Models
- **Time Series Forecasting**: ARIMA, Exponential Smoothing, Prophet
- **Demand Forecasting**: Predict future demand for products/services
- **Financial Forecasting**: Revenue, cost, and profit predictions
- **Resource Planning**: Predict resource requirements

#### 2. Machine Learning Models
- **Supervised Learning**: Classification and regression models
- **Unsupervised Learning**: Clustering and dimensionality reduction
- **Ensemble Methods**: Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, XGBoost
- **Deep Learning**: Neural networks for complex pattern recognition

#### 3. Risk Analytics
- **Credit Risk**: Assess creditworthiness and default probability
- **Market Risk**: Analyze market volatility and risk exposure
- **Operational Risk**: Identify and quantify operational risks
- **Fraud Detection**: Detect fraudulent activities and transactions

### Prescriptive Analytics

#### 1. Optimization Models
- **Linear Programming**: Optimize resource allocation
- **Integer Programming**: Optimize discrete decision variables
- **Nonlinear Programming**: Handle complex optimization problems
- **Multi-objective Optimization**: Balance multiple objectives

#### 2. Simulation Models
- **Monte Carlo Simulation**: Model uncertainty and risk
- **Discrete Event Simulation**: Model complex business processes
- **Agent-Based Modeling**: Model individual behavior and interactions
- **Scenario Analysis**: Test different business scenarios

#### 3. Decision Support Systems
- **What-If Analysis**: Test different scenarios and assumptions
- **Sensitivity Analysis**: Understand impact of parameter changes
- **Decision Trees**: Visualize decision paths and outcomes
- **Recommendation Engines**: Provide personalized recommendations
