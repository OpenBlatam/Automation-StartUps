## ðŸŽ¯ Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Primary Onboarding Metrics

#### Time to Productivity
- **Definition**: Days from start date to first meaningful contribution
- **Target**: 30-45 days for AI companies
- **Measurement**: Project completion, code commits, customer interactions
- **Formula**: (Date of First Contribution - Start Date) in days

#### 90-Day Retention Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of new hires still employed after 90 days
- **Target**: 85-95% for AI companies
- **Measurement**: Employee status at 90-day mark
- **Formula**: (Employees Still Employed at 90 Days / Total New Hires) Ã— 100

#### New Hire Satisfaction Score
- **Definition**: Average satisfaction rating from new hire surveys
- **Target**: 4.5+ out of 5.0
- **Measurement**: 30, 60, and 90-day surveys
- **Formula**: Average of all satisfaction ratings

#### Manager Satisfaction Score
- **Definition**: Average rating from managers on new hire performance
- **Target**: 4.0+ out of 5.0
- **Measurement**: Manager feedback surveys
- **Formula**: Average of all manager ratings

### AI-Specific Metrics

#### AI Tool Proficiency Score
- **Definition**: Average proficiency rating across AI tools and platforms
- **Target**: 4.0+ out of 5.0
- **Measurement**: Technical assessments and tool usage analytics
- **Formula**: Average of all AI tool proficiency ratings

#### Technical Skill Development Rate
- **Definition**: Rate of technical skill improvement over time
- **Target**: 20% improvement per month
- **Measurement**: Skills assessments and project complexity
- **Formula**: (Current Skill Level - Initial Skill Level) / Initial Skill Level Ã— 100

#### Innovation Contribution Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of new hires contributing to innovation projects
- **Target**: 60%+ within 90 days
- **Measurement**: Innovation project participation
- **Formula**: (New Hires in Innovation Projects / Total New Hires) Ã— 100

#### AI Ethics Understanding Score
- **Definition**: Average score on AI ethics and responsible development assessments
- **Target**: 4.5+ out of 5.0
- **Measurement**: Ethics training assessments
- **Formula**: Average of all ethics assessment scores
