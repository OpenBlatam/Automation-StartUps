## üéØ **RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT FRAMEWORK**

### **The 5-Stage Investor Relationship Lifecycle**

#### **Stage 1: Initial Contact (0-30 days)**
- **Goal:** Create positive first impression
- **Focus:** Credibility building, value demonstration
- **Activities:** Initial outreach, pitch presentation, follow-up
- **Success Metrics:** Meeting scheduled, interest generated

#### **Stage 2: Due Diligence (30-90 days)**
- **Goal:** Build trust and confidence
- **Focus:** Transparency, responsiveness, value-add
- **Activities:** Reference calls, technical deep-dive, financial review
- **Success Metrics:** Due diligence completed, terms discussed

#### **Stage 3: Investment Decision (90-120 days)**
- **Goal:** Secure investment commitment
- **Focus:** Negotiation, relationship building, mutual value
- **Activities:** Term sheet negotiation, legal process, closing
- **Success Metrics:** Investment closed, partnership established

#### **Stage 4: Active Partnership (1-3 years)**
- **Goal:** Maximize value creation
- **Focus:** Growth, support, strategic guidance
- **Activities:** Board meetings, strategic planning, introductions
- **Success Metrics:** Company growth, investor satisfaction

#### **Stage 5: Exit and Beyond (3+ years)**
- **Goal:** Successful exit and ongoing relationship
- **Focus:** Exit strategy, value realization, future opportunities
- **Activities:** Exit planning, transaction support, future investments
- **Success Metrics:** Successful exit, ongoing relationship

## üé™ **ADVANCED RELATIONSHIP TECHNIQUES**

### **Technique 1: The Value-First Approach**
```
HOW IT WORKS: Provide value before asking for anything
IMPLEMENTATION:
- Share market insights
- Provide strategic advice
- Offer network access
- Give before asking
- Create mutual value

SCRIPT:
"Before we discuss anything else, let me share some market insights that might be valuable for your portfolio.
I've been following your investments in [area], and I think our platform could help several of your portfolio companies improve their marketing.
I'd love to get your thoughts on this market opportunity, and I'm happy to share our insights with your team.
This isn't just about funding; it's about creating mutual value."
```

### **Technique 2: The Partnership Approach**
```
HOW IT WORKS: Focus on partnership and mutual value
IMPLEMENTATION:
- Emphasize partnership
- Show mutual benefit
- Demonstrate value-add
- Create win-win scenario
- Build long-term relationship

SCRIPT:
"This isn't just about funding; it's about partnership. We're looking for investors who can help us:
1. Scale globally and access new markets
2. Build strategic partnerships with your portfolio companies
3. Recruit top talent from your network
4. Navigate regulatory challenges in different markets
5. Prepare for our eventual exit
Based on your background and network, I believe you'd be an incredible partner."
```

### **Technique 3: The Transparency Approach**
```
HOW IT WORKS: Be transparent and honest about challenges
IMPLEMENTATION:
- Share challenges openly
- Show vulnerability appropriately
- Demonstrate integrity
- Build trust through honesty
- Create authentic relationship

SCRIPT:
"I want to be completely transparent about our challenges and opportunities.
We're facing [specific challenge], but here's how we're addressing it.
Our customers are seeing [specific results], which validates our approach.
We're committed to building something great together, and I believe transparency is the foundation of a strong partnership."
```

### **Technique 4: The Strategic Approach**
```
HOW IT WORKS: Focus on strategic alignment and long-term value
IMPLEMENTATION:
- Show strategic fit
- Demonstrate long-term thinking
- Align with investor goals
- Create strategic value
- Build strategic partnership

SCRIPT:
"Based on your investment thesis in [area], I believe we're a perfect strategic fit.
Our technology aligns with your focus on [specific area], and we can create significant value together.
This investment would be consistent with your track record of backing companies that transform their industries.
How can we work together to create mutual strategic value?"
```

### **Technique 5: The Network Approach**
```
HOW IT WORKS: Leverage investor network for mutual benefit
IMPLEMENTATION:
- Show network value
- Demonstrate mutual benefit
- Create network opportunities
- Build network relationships
- Leverage network access

SCRIPT:
"Your network in [area] would be incredibly valuable as we scale globally.
We could help your portfolio companies improve their marketing, and you could help us access new markets.
```
