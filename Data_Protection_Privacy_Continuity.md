## Executive Summary

## Data Protection Framework

### Regulatory Compliance

#### General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- **Scope:** EU customer data protection
- **Key Requirements:** Data breach notification within 72 hours
- **Business Impact:** Critical for EU operations
- **Compliance Officer:** Data Protection Officer (DPO)
- **Legal Review:** Legal Counsel

**Critical Requirements:**
- Data breach notification procedures
- Data subject rights management
- Privacy impact assessments
- Data processing agreements
- Cross-border data transfer compliance
- Consent management
- Data retention policies
- Right to be forgotten

#### California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- **Scope:** California resident data protection
- **Key Requirements:** Consumer rights and data transparency
- **Business Impact:** High for US operations
- **Compliance Officer:** Privacy Officer
- **Legal Review:** Legal Counsel

**Critical Requirements:**
- Consumer rights management
- Data transparency requirements
- Opt-out mechanisms
- Data deletion procedures
- Third-party data sharing controls
- Privacy policy updates
- Consumer request handling

#### Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)
- **Scope:** Canadian customer data protection
- **Key Requirements:** Privacy protection and consent
- **Business Impact:** Medium for Canadian operations
- **Compliance Officer:** Privacy Officer
- **Legal Review:** Legal Counsel

**Critical Requirements:**
- Privacy protection measures
- Consent management
- Data breach notification
- Privacy impact assessments
- Data retention policies
- Cross-border data transfers

### Data Classification

#### Public Data
- **Definition:** Information that can be freely shared
- **Examples:** Marketing materials, public announcements
- **Protection Level:** Basic
- **Access Control:** Open access
- **Retention:** Indefinite

#### Internal Data
- **Definition:** Information for internal use only
- **Examples:** Internal procedures, employee information
- **Protection Level:** Standard
- **Access Control:** Employee access
- **Retention:** 7 years

#### Confidential Data
- **Definition:** Sensitive business information
- **Examples:** Financial data, business strategies
- **Protection Level:** High
- **Access Control:** Restricted access
- **Retention:** 10 years

#### Restricted Data
- **Definition:** Highly sensitive information
- **Examples:** Personal data, financial records
- **Protection Level:** Maximum
- **Access Control:** Limited access
- **Retention:** As required by law

1. **Customer Data**
   - [ ] Active customers: 7 years
   - [ ] Inactive customers: 3 years
   - [ ] Marketing data: 2 years
