## E) CUSTOMER INTERVIEWS (3 Strategic Questions)

### Power User Interview Protocol

1. **"What specific document generation challenge does our bulk AI platform solve that DocuSign or PandaDoc couldn't handle?"**
   - *Purpose*: Validate unique value proposition and differentiation
   - *Success Criteria*: Clear competitive advantage identified
   - *Red Flag*: Generic answers or easy substitution

2. **"How much time and money does our platform save you per month, and what would happen if we increased pricing by 50%?"**
   - *Purpose*: Assess quantifiable value delivery and price sensitivity
   - *Success Criteria*: Clear ROI demonstration, price elasticity understanding
   - *Red Flag*: Vague benefits or high price sensitivity

3. **"What's your biggest concern about using AI for legal/business documents, and how do we address it?"**
   - *Purpose*: Identify adoption barriers and trust factors
   - *Success Criteria*: Clear concerns identified with mitigation strategies
   - *Red Flag*: Unaddressed legal or accuracy concerns
