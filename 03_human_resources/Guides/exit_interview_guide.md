## üìã Interview Overview

### Purpose
The exit interview serves multiple important purposes:

- **Gather Feedback**: Understand the employee's experience and reasons for leaving
- **Identify Improvement Areas**: Learn about potential issues or concerns
- **Maintain Relationships**: Preserve positive relationships for potential future opportunities
- **Knowledge Transfer**: Ensure smooth transition and knowledge preservation
- **Process Improvement**: Use feedback to improve company policies and practices

### Interview Philosophy
We conduct exit interviews with the following principles:

- **Confidentiality**: All feedback is treated confidentially and professionally
- **Honesty**: We encourage honest and constructive feedback
- **Respect**: We respect the employee's decision and maintain professionalism
- **Learning**: We use feedback to improve our workplace and processes
- **Support**: We provide support during the transition process

## üéØ Pre-Interview Preparation

### HR Preparation
- [ ] **Review Employee File**: Check performance history, reviews, and any previous concerns
- [ ] **Gather Information**: Collect relevant data about the employee's role and contributions
- [ ] **Prepare Questions**: Customize questions based on the employee's specific situation
- [ ] **Schedule Interview**: Set up convenient time and location for the interview
- [ ] **Notify Manager**: Inform manager of interview schedule and purpose

### Employee Preparation
- [ ] **Complete Pre-Interview Form**: Fill out preliminary exit interview questionnaire
- [ ] **Gather Thoughts**: Reflect on experience and prepare honest feedback
- [ ] **Review Questions**: Familiarize with potential interview topics
- [ ] **Prepare Examples**: Think of specific examples to support feedback
- [ ] **Consider Suggestions**: Prepare constructive suggestions for improvement

### Interview Setup
- [ ] **Private Location**: Ensure confidential and comfortable interview space
- [ ] **Adequate Time**: Allow sufficient time for comprehensive discussion
- [ ] **Recording Equipment**: Set up recording if employee consents
- [ ] **Documentation**: Prepare forms and note-taking materials
- [ ] **Follow-up Plan**: Prepare plan for post-interview actions

## üìù Interview Questions & Topics

### Opening Questions
1. **How are you feeling about your decision to leave?**
   - Understanding the employee's emotional state
   - Gauging confidence in their decision
   - Identifying any potential for retention

2. **What prompted your decision to leave?**
   - Primary reasons for departure
   - Timeline of decision-making process
   - Factors that influenced the decision

3. **How long have you been considering this decision?**
   - Timeline of consideration
   - Events that may have triggered the decision
   - Opportunity for early intervention

### Work Experience & Role

#### **Job Satisfaction**
4. **What did you enjoy most about your role?**
   - Positive aspects of the position

   - Strengths of the role

5. **What did you find most challenging about your role?**
   - Difficult aspects of the position
   - Areas that caused frustration
   - Skills or resources that were lacking

6. **How well did your role match your expectations?**

#### **AI Course Platform Experience**
