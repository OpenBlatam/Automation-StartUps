### Performance Benchmarks

- **Tier 1 (Basic):** 99.0% uptime (87.6 hours downtime/year)
- **Tier 2 (Redundant):** 99.9% uptime (8.76 hours downtime/year)
- **Tier 3 (Concurrently Maintainable):** 99.99% uptime (52.56 minutes downtime/year)
- **Tier 4 (Fault Tolerant):** 99.999% uptime (5.26 minutes downtime/year)
- **Tier 5 (Resilient):** 99.9999% uptime (31.5 seconds downtime/year)
- **Tier 6 (Ultra-Resilient):** 99.99999% uptime (3.15 seconds downtime/year)
