#### **3. SMB Companies (Priority 3)**
- **Rationale**: Large volume, lower acquisition cost
- **Size**: 150,000 companies
- **Approach**: Self-service with minimal support
- **Investment**: 20% of marketing budget

#### **4. Solo Entrepreneurs (Priority 4)**
- **Rationale**: High volume, low maintenance
- **Size**: 500,000 individuals
- **Approach**: Freemium model with upgrades
- **Investment**: 5% of marketing budget

### **Go-to-Market Strategy by Segment**

#### **Enterprise Strategy**
- **Sales Model**: Direct sales with dedicated account managers
- **Marketing**: Account-based marketing, industry events
- **Product**: Enterprise features, security, compliance
- **Pricing**: Custom pricing, annual contracts
- **Success Metrics**: Deal size, retention, expansion

#### **Mid-Market Strategy**
- **Sales Model**: Inside sales with product-led growth
- **Marketing**: Content marketing, webinars, trials
- **Product**: Core features, integrations, scalability
- **Pricing**: Tiered pricing, monthly/annual options
- **Success Metrics**: Conversion rate, time to value, expansion

#### **SMB Strategy**
- **Sales Model**: Self-service with minimal sales support
- **Marketing**: Digital marketing, SEO, social media
- **Product**: Essential features, ease of use
- **Pricing**: Simple pricing, monthly billing
- **Success Metrics**: Signup rate, activation, retention

#### **Solo Entrepreneur Strategy**
- **Sales Model**: Freemium with upgrade prompts
- **Marketing**: Social media, content, community
- **Product**: Basic features, templates, automation
- **Pricing**: Freemium, low-cost upgrades
- **Success Metrics**: Free-to-paid conversion, usage
