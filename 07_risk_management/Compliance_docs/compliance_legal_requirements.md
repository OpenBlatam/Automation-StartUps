## Compliance Procedures

### Data Breach Response

#### Immediate Response (0-24 hours)
1. **Incident Assessment**
   - Identify breach scope and impact
   - Assess data types and volumes affected
   - Determine regulatory notification requirements
   - Document breach details and timeline

2. **Legal Notification**
   - Contact legal counsel immediately
   - Assess regulatory notification requirements
   - Prepare notification documents
   - Coordinate with regulatory authorities

3. **Internal Communication**
   - Notify management team
   - Activate incident response team
   - Coordinate with legal and compliance teams
   - Document all communications

#### Short-term Response (24-72 hours)
1. **Regulatory Notification**
   - Submit required regulatory notifications
   - Provide detailed breach information
   - Coordinate with regulatory authorities
   - Document all regulatory communications

2. **Customer Notification**
   - Prepare customer notification letters
   - Coordinate customer communication
   - Provide breach response information
   - Document customer communications

3. **Investigation and Remediation**
   - Conduct thorough breach investigation
   - Implement immediate security measures
   - Coordinate with law enforcement if needed
   - Document all remediation efforts

#### Long-term Response (72+ hours)
1. **Ongoing Compliance**
   - Monitor regulatory requirements
   - Provide additional information as requested
   - Coordinate with regulatory authorities
   - Document ongoing compliance efforts

2. **Legal Proceedings**
   - Coordinate with legal counsel
   - Prepare for potential litigation
   - Document all legal proceedings
   - Manage legal costs and resources
