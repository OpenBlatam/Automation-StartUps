## Escalation Matrix

### Level 1: Immediate Response (0-15 minutes)
**Trigger:** Critical system failure, security breach, major outage

**Primary Contacts:**
- CTO (Technical issues)
- Operations Manager (Operational issues)
- Security Officer (Security incidents)

**Actions:**
1. Immediate notification to primary contact
2. Activate incident response team
3. Begin initial assessment
4. Notify stakeholders

### Level 2: Management Response (15-60 minutes)
**Trigger:** Level 1 escalation or significant business impact

**Primary Contacts:**
- CEO (Business-critical issues)
- COO (Operational issues)
- CTO (Technical issues)

**Actions:**
1. Management notification
2. Resource allocation
3. External communication
4. Business impact assessment

### Level 3: Executive Response (1-4 hours)
**Trigger:** Level 2 escalation or major business disruption

**Primary Contacts:**
- CEO
- Board of Directors (if applicable)
- Legal Counsel (if needed)

**Actions:**
1. Executive decision making
2. External stakeholder communication
3. Legal and compliance review
4. Business continuity activation

### Level 4: Crisis Management (4+ hours)
**Trigger:** Level 3 escalation or catastrophic failure

**Primary Contacts:**
- CEO
- Board of Directors
- External crisis management team
- Legal Counsel
- Public Relations (if needed)

**Actions:**
1. Crisis management team activation
2. External communication strategy
3. Business recovery planning
4. Stakeholder management

## Scenario-Specific Escalation

### IT Infrastructure Outage
1. **Level 1:** DevOps Lead → CTO
2. **Level 2:** CTO → CEO
3. **Level 3:** CEO → Board
4. **Level 4:** Board → External Crisis Team

### Security Breach
1. **Level 1:** Security Officer → CTO
2. **Level 2:** CTO → CEO
3. **Level 3:** CEO → Legal Counsel
4. **Level 4:** Legal → External Security Team

### Webinar Failure
1. **Level 1:** Webinar Operations → Education Director
2. **Level 2:** Education Director → COO
3. **Level 3:** COO → CEO
4. **Level 4:** CEO → External PR Team

### SaaS Platform Outage
1. **Level 1:** DevOps Lead → CTO
2. **Level 2:** CTO → CEO
3. **Level 3:** CEO → Customer Success
4. **Level 4:** Customer Success → External Support

### AI Service Failure
1. **Level 1:** AI Operations Manager → CTO
2. **Level 2:** CTO → CEO
3. **Level 3:** CEO → Technical Partners
4. **Level 4:** Technical Partners → External AI Providers

## Communication Protocols

### Internal Communication
- **Primary:** Slack channels (#emergency, #incident-response)
- **Secondary:** Email + Phone calls
- **Emergency:** SMS alerts via PagerDuty
- **Escalation:** Direct phone calls to management

### External Communication
- **Customers:** Status page + email notifications
- **Partners:** Direct communication via established channels
- **Vendors:** Support tickets + phone calls
- **Media:** PR team (if applicable)

### Communication Templates

#### Level 1 Alert
```
Subject: URGENT - [Service] Critical Issue

[Contact Name],

We're experiencing a critical issue with [Service].

Issue: [Description]
Impact: [Scope]
Status: [Current Status]
ETA: [Resolution Time]

Please respond immediately.

[Your Name]
```

#### Level 2 Escalation
```
Subject: ESCALATION - [Service] Issue Requires Management Attention

[Management Name],

The following issue has been escalated:

Original Issue: [Description]
Current Status: [Status]
Business Impact: [Impact]
Required Actions: [Actions]

Please provide guidance on next steps.

[Your Name]
```

#### Level 3 Executive Alert
```
Subject: EXECUTIVE ALERT - [Service] Major Business Impact

[Executive Name],

We have a major business impact situation:

Issue: [Description]
Business Impact: [Impact]
Financial Impact: [Cost]
Timeline: [Resolution Time]
Required Decisions: [Decisions]

Immediate action required.

[Your Name]
```

## Emergency Procedures

### Contact Verification
1. **Daily:** Verify contact information accuracy
2. **Weekly:** Test communication channels
3. **Monthly:** Update contact information
4. **Quarterly:** Review escalation procedures

### Communication Testing
1. **Monthly:** Test all communication channels
2. **Quarterly:** Conduct escalation drills
3. **Annually:** Full communication system test
4. **As Needed:** Update procedures based on lessons learned

### Backup Communication

- **Secondary:** Email
- **Tertiary:** SMS
- **Emergency:** In-person (if possible)
