## 4 Common Objections & Talking Points

### 1. **PRICING TRANSPARENCY & VALUE CONCERNS**

**Common Objection:** *"Lever seems expensive and the pricing structure is unclear"*

**Talking Points:**
- **Customized Pricing Model:** "I understand pricing transparency is important. Lever offers tailored pricing based on your specific needs and team size, ensuring you only pay for what you actually use. Let me walk you through our flexible pricing structure."

- **ROI Demonstration:** "Our customers typically see a return on investment within 13 months. This happens through reduced time-to-hire, improved candidate quality, and streamlined processes. Would you like me to show you how we calculate ROI for companies similar to yours?"

- **All-in-One Solution:** "Lever combines both ATS and CRM functionality in one platform. This means you can eliminate the need for multiple tools, which often results in significant cost savings compared to using separate systems."

**Value Proposition:** "Think of Lever as an investment in your team's efficiency rather than just another software expense."

---

### 2. **CUSTOMIZATION & FLEXIBILITY LIMITATIONS**

**Common Objection:** *"We need more customization options for our unique workflows"*

**Talking Points:**
- **Flexible Pipeline Creation:** "Lever allows you to create completely customized recruitment pipelines for different departments, roles, or hiring processes. You can set up as many stages as needed and customize them to match your exact workflow."

- **Advanced Reporting with Data Explorer:** "Our Data Explorer add-on provides powerful custom reporting capabilities. You can create dashboards and reports that give you exactly the insights you need to make data-driven hiring decisions."

- **Continuous Platform Evolution:** "We're committed to evolving based on customer feedback. Our product team regularly introduces new features and enhancements. What specific customizations are most important for your team?"

**Value Proposition:** "Lever grows with your organization and adapts to your changing needs."

---

### 3. **USER EXPERIENCE & LEARNING CURVE**

**Common Objection:** *"The platform seems complex and our team might struggle to adopt it"*

**Talking Points:**
- **Intuitive Design:** "Lever features a clean, modern interface designed with user experience in mind. Our customers tell us it's one of the most intuitive ATS platforms they've used. Let me show you the interface during our demo."

- **Comprehensive Onboarding:** "We provide structured implementation support to ensure your team gets up and running quickly. Our onboarding process is designed to minimize disruption to your current workflow."

- **Dedicated Support Ecosystem:** "You'll have access to dedicated customer success managers, comprehensive Help Center, email support, live chat, and our community forum. We're here to support your team every step of the way."

**Value Proposition:** "Adoption should be smooth and supported, not a burden on your team."

---

### 4. **SYSTEM PERFORMANCE & RELIABILITY**

**Common Objection:** *"I've heard there can be performance issues and lag"*

**Talking Points:**
- **Proactive Performance Management:** "We continuously monitor and optimize system performance. While no platform is perfect, we're committed to providing the most reliable experience possible and quickly address any issues that arise."

- **Real-Time Monitoring:** "Our engineering team uses proactive system monitoring to identify and resolve performance issues before they impact your workflow."

- **Customer Feedback Integration:** "We take user feedback seriously and use it to drive continuous improvements in system reliability and speed. Your experience matters to us."

**Value Proposition:** "Reliability is our priority, and we're constantly working to improve it."

## Advanced Objection Handling

### "We're locked into a contract"
**Response:** "I understand contract commitments. What's your renewal date? I'd be happy to show you how Lever can prepare you for a smooth transition when you're ready. Many of our customers start with a pilot program to see the value before making a full commitment."

**Response:** "That's a valid concern. Let me connect you with our technical team to discuss integration requirements. We have pre-built integrations with most major systems and can provide detailed technical documentation. What systems are you currently using?"

### "We need to see more customer references"

### "The price is higher than our budget"
**Response:** "I understand budget constraints. Let me show you how we can structure this to fit your budget. We offer flexible payment terms, and many customers find the ROI pays for itself within the first year. What's your target budget range?"

## Advanced Sales Psychology Techniques

### Building Trust & Credibility
**Technique:** "I've been in your shoes before..."
**Application:** "I used to be an HR Director at a company similar to yours, and I faced the exact same challenges you're describing. That's why I'm so passionate about helping companies like yours solve these problems."

**Technique:** Social Proof Stacking

### Creating Urgency Without Pressure
**Technique:** Scarcity with Value
**Application:** "We have limited implementation slots for Q1, but more importantly, every month you wait is costing you [calculated amount] in inefficiencies. Let me show you what that looks like..."

**Technique:** Opportunity Cost
**Application:** "While you're evaluating options, you're missing out on [specific benefits]. The companies that implement now are already seeing [specific results]. What's the cost of waiting?"

- Start with high-value features to establish perception
- Use premium pricing as anchor, then show value alternatives
- Present best-case scenarios first

**Loss Aversion:**
- Emphasize what they'll lose by not acting
- Show cost of inaction vs. cost of action
- Frame benefits as avoiding losses rather than gaining benefits

**Social Proof:**
- Use customer logos and testimonials
- Share success stories from similar companies
- Leverage industry recognition and awards

**Authority Bias:**
- Reference industry experts and thought leaders
- Use data from recognized research firms
- Leverage customer references from respected companies

## Advanced Objection Handling Mastery

### The Complete Objection Handling Arsenal
**Price Objections:**
- **"It's too expensive"** → "I understand budget is important. Let me show you the ROI calculation. Most customers see a return within 6 months."
- **"We don't have budget"** → "What would need to happen for budget to become available? Sometimes the cost of inaction is higher than the investment."
- **"We need to get approval"** → "Who else is involved in the budget approval process? I can help you build a business case."

**Timing Objections:**
- **"It's not the right time"** → "What would need to happen for this to become a priority? The companies that act now are already seeing results."
- **"We're too busy"** → "I understand you're busy. That's exactly why this solution exists - to save you time and reduce your workload."
- **"We need to think about it"** → "What specific information would help you make this decision? I can provide additional resources or arrange a reference call."

**Competitive Objections:**
- **"We're evaluating other options"** → "That's smart. What criteria are you using to compare solutions? I'd be happy to provide a detailed comparison."
- **"We're happy with our current system"** → "That's great to hear. What I'd love to understand is - are there any areas where your current system falls short?"

**Authority Objections:**
- **"I need to discuss with my team"** → "Who else would be involved in this decision? I can help you build a business case that addresses their concerns."
- **"I'm not the decision maker"** → "Who is the decision maker? I'd love to understand the decision-making process and how I can support you."

### Advanced Objection Prevention Strategies
**Proactive Objection Prevention:**
- **Address concerns before they arise:** "I know budget is always a consideration, so let me show you the ROI first"
- **Use social proof early:** "Most companies similar to yours see a 40% improvement in time-to-hire"
- **Set proper expectations:** "Our implementation typically takes 6-8 weeks, and we handle all the technical setup"
- **Build credibility:** "We've helped over 5,000 companies streamline their hiring process"
