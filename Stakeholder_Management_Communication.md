#### Operational Teams

- **Enterprise Customers:** Large business customers
- **SMB Customers:** Small and medium business customers

#### Business Partners
- **Technology Partners:** Technology and service providers
- **Channel Partners:** Sales and distribution partners

- **Vendor Partners:** Vendor and supplier partners

#### Regulatory Stakeholders

#### Media and Public

- **Analysts:** Industry analysts and consultants
