## Executive Summary

This document provides comprehensive training materials and knowledge base resources for implementing and maintaining our business continuity plan across all team members and stakeholders.

---

## Training Program Overview

### Training Objectives
- **Awareness:** Ensure all team members understand BCP importance
- **Competency:** Develop necessary skills for BCP implementation
- **Readiness:** Prepare teams for emergency response
- **Compliance:** Meet regulatory and legal requirements
- **Continuous Improvement:** Maintain and improve BCP effectiveness

### Training Levels

#### Level 1: Awareness Training
- **Target Audience:** All employees
- **Duration:** 2 hours
- **Frequency:** Annually
- **Content:** Basic BCP concepts and procedures

#### Level 2: Operational Training
- **Target Audience:** Operations team members
- **Duration:** 8 hours
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Content:** Detailed operational procedures

#### Level 3: Technical Training
- **Target Audience:** Technical team members
- **Duration:** 16 hours
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Content:** Technical recovery procedures

#### Level 4: Management Training
- **Target Audience:** Management team
- **Duration:** 4 hours
- **Frequency:** Semi-annually
- **Content:** Leadership and decision-making
