# Enhanced VC Screening Framework
## $250M Early-Stage Fund Focus: AI, Climate, Fintech

### Comprehensive Evaluation Criteria

#### 1. PROBLEM ASSESSMENT (25% weight)
**Market Pain & Size**
- **Pain Intensity**: How acute is the problem? (1-10)
  - 10: Critical, life-threatening, regulatory mandate
  - 7-9: Significant operational/financial impact
  - 4-6: Moderate inconvenience/cost
  - 1-3: Nice-to-have improvement

- **Market Size**: TAM/SAM/SOM analysis (1-10)
  - 10: $100B+ TAM, clear path to $1B+ revenue
  - 7-9: $10-100B TAM, $100M+ potential
  - 4-6: $1-10B TAM, $10-100M potential
  - 1-3: <$1B TAM, limited upside

- **Market Timing**: Is the market ready? (1-10)
  - 10: Market is actively seeking solutions
  - 7-9: Early adopters showing strong demand
  - 4-6: Market awareness building
  - 1-3: Market not ready/doesn't exist yet

#### 2. SOLUTION ANALYSIS (20% weight)
**Defensibility & Moat**
- **Technical Moat**: Proprietary technology/IP (1-10)
  - 10: Patent-protected, breakthrough innovation
  - 7-9: Significant technical barriers
  - 4-6: Some differentiation
  - 1-3: Commoditized solution

- **Network Effects**: Value increases with users (1-10)
  - 10: Strong network effects, winner-take-all
  - 7-9: Moderate network effects
  - 4-6: Some user value increase
  - 1-3: No network effects

- **Data Moat**: Proprietary data advantage (1-10)
  - 10: Unique, valuable, hard-to-replicate data
  - 7-9: Good data assets
  - 4-6: Some data advantage
  - 1-3: No data moat

- **Switching Costs**: Customer lock-in (1-10)
  - 10: High switching costs, sticky customers
  - 7-9: Moderate switching costs
  - 4-6: Some switching costs
  - 1-3: Easy to switch

#### 3. TRACTION METRICS (20% weight)
**Growth & Revenue**
- **MRR Growth**: Month-over-month growth (1-10)
  - 10: >20% MoM consistently
  - 7-9: 10-20% MoM
  - 4-6: 5-10% MoM
  - 1-3: <5% MoM or declining

- **User Growth**: Customer acquisition rate (1-10)
  - 10: Viral growth, >50% MoM
  - 7-9: Strong growth, 20-50% MoM
  - 4-6: Steady growth, 5-20% MoM
  - 1-3: Slow/declining growth

- **Revenue Quality**: Recurring vs. one-time (1-10)
  - 10: Strong recurring revenue, high retention
  - 7-9: Mostly recurring, good retention
  - 4-6: Mixed revenue model
  - 1-3: One-time sales, poor retention

- **Market Validation**: Customer feedback & adoption (1-10)
  - 10: Strong NPS, reference customers, expansion
  - 7-9: Positive feedback, growing usage
  - 4-6: Mixed feedback
  - 1-3: Poor feedback, low adoption

#### 4. TEAM EVALUATION (15% weight)
**Founder-Market Fit**
- **Domain Expertise**: Relevant experience (1-10)
  - 10: Deep domain expertise, industry veterans
  - 7-9: Strong relevant experience
  - 4-6: Some relevant experience
  - 1-3: Limited/no relevant experience

- **Execution Track Record**: Prior successes (1-10)
  - 10: Multiple exits, proven operators
  - 7-9: Previous startup experience
  - 4-6: Corporate success, management roles
  - 1-3: First-time founders

- **Team Completeness**: Key roles filled (1-10)
  - 10: Complete founding team, complementary skills
  - 7-9: Most key roles covered
  - 4-6: Some gaps but addressable
  - 1-3: Major gaps, incomplete team

- **Leadership Quality**: Vision, communication, adaptability (1-10)
  - 10: Exceptional leaders, clear vision
  - 7-9: Strong leadership qualities
  - 4-6: Adequate leadership
  - 1-3: Weak leadership, unclear vision

#### 5. UNIT ECONOMICS (10% weight)
**Financial Health**
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Cost to acquire customer (1-10)
  - 10: CAC <$100, efficient channels
  - 7-9: CAC $100-500, reasonable
  - 4-6: CAC $500-2000, concerning
  - 1-3: CAC >$2000, unsustainable

- **Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Customer value over time (1-10)
  - 10: LTV >$10K, high value customers
  - 7-9: LTV $2K-10K, good value
  - 4-6: LTV $500-2K, moderate value
  - 1-3: LTV <$500, low value

- **LTV/CAC Ratio**: Unit economics efficiency (1-10)
  - 10: LTV/CAC >10x, excellent
  - 7-9: LTV/CAC 5-10x, good
  - 4-6: LTV/CAC 3-5x, acceptable
  - 1-3: LTV/CAC <3x, poor

- **Burn Rate**: Monthly cash consumption (1-10)
  - 10: <$50K/month, efficient
  - 7-9: $50-200K/month, reasonable
  - 4-6: $200-500K/month, high
  - 1-3: >$500K/month, unsustainable

#### 6. FUNDING ASK (5% weight)
**Valuation & Use of Funds**
- **Valuation Reasonableness**: Fair market value (1-10)
  - 10: Undervalued, great deal
  - 7-9: Fair valuation
  - 4-6: Slightly overvalued
  - 1-3: Significantly overvalued

- **Use of Funds**: Clear, logical allocation (1-10)
  - 10: Clear plan, growth-focused
  - 7-9: Reasonable allocation
  - 4-6: Some unclear uses
  - 1-3: Unclear/poor use of funds

- **Runway**: Time to next milestone (1-10)
  - 10: 18+ months runway
  - 7-9: 12-18 months runway
  - 4-6: 6-12 months runway
  - 1-3: <6 months runway

#### 7. RED FLAGS ASSESSMENT (5% weight)
**Risk Factors**
- **Legal/Regulatory**: Compliance issues (1-10)
  - 10: No red flags, clean
  - 7-9: Minor concerns
  - 4-6: Some issues
  - 1-3: Major red flags

- **Competition**: Market saturation/threats (1-10)
  - 10: Blue ocean, first mover
  - 7-9: Some competition, differentiated
  - 4-6: Competitive market
  - 1-3: Crowded, commoditized

- **Technology Risk**: Technical feasibility (1-10)
  - 10: Proven technology, low risk
  - 7-9: Some technical challenges
  - 4-6: Moderate technical risk
  - 1-3: High technical risk

- **Market Risk**: External factors (1-10)
  - 10: Stable market conditions
  - 7-9: Some market volatility
  - 4-6: Moderate market risk
  - 1-3: High market risk

### SCORING METHODOLOGY

**Weighted Score Calculation:**
- Problem: 25%
- Solution: 20%
- Traction: 20%
- Team: 15%
- Unit Economics: 10%
- Ask: 5%
- Red Flags: 5%

**Overall Score Ranges:**
- **9-10**: Exceptional opportunity (Pass)
- **7-8**: Strong opportunity (Deep Dive)
- **5-6**: Moderate opportunity (Deep Dive with caution)
- **3-4**: Weak opportunity (Reject)
- **1-2**: Poor opportunity (Reject)

### DECISION FRAMEWORK

**PASS Criteria:**
- Overall score ≥ 8.0
- No major red flags (score ≥ 7)
- Strong traction (score ≥ 7)
- Clear path to $100M+ revenue

**DEEP DIVE Criteria:**
- Overall score 6.0-7.9
- Some concerns but addressable
- Potential for strong returns
- Requires detailed due diligence

**REJECT Criteria:**
- Overall score < 6.0
- Major red flags present
- Poor unit economics
- Limited market opportunity
- Weak team or execution risk

### SECTOR-SPECIFIC CONSIDERATIONS

**AI Startups:**
- Focus on data moats and technical differentiation
- Evaluate AI talent and research capabilities
- Consider regulatory implications
- Assess scalability of AI models

**Climate Startups:**
- Evaluate regulatory tailwinds
- Consider carbon credit markets
- Assess technology readiness level (TRL)
- Factor in government incentives

**Fintech Startups:**
- Evaluate regulatory compliance
- Assess security and risk management
- Consider banking partnerships
- Factor in customer trust and adoption
